Eman Get Dough Releases "Under The Influence" LP

2018-06-20 — news

After the buzz created by the singles "Money Thru The Roof" featuring Hoodrich Pablo Juan and "Digital Dash," North Miami-native Eman Get Dough releases his 12-song breakthrough hopeful effort Under The Influence.In 2011, Eman and his cousin were involved in a severe automobile accident that caused him temporary paralysis and resulted in his cousin to losing his life. "Once I got back into the swing of things, making music became my passion," Eman says. That drive has steadily increased in acceleration.Now, after six months of feverish promotion, Under The Influence serves as the appetizer to a string of releases coming later this year in collaboration with Freebandz Casino, the CEO of Freebandz and brother of "Mask Off" legend Future.Check out the stream, below.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QFIY4XK4kuk[apple_news_ad type="standard"]
