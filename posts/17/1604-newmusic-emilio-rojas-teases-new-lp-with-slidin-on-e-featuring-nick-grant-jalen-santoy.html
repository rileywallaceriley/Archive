#NewMusic: Emilio Rojas Teases New LP with ‘Slidin’ On E’ Featuring Nick Grant & Jalen Santoy

Thu, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Emilio Rojas is gearing up for his new album, It Always Gets Better, set to drop on August 2, 2024.

To get fans excited, he's releasing a brand new single, "Slidin' On E," featuring Nick Grant and Jalen Santoy.

This fiery track is a standalone release and won't be included on the upcoming album.

Additionally, Emilio has dropped a new video for his "First Person Shooter" freestyle, showcasing his impressive lyrical skills with lines like "They Play Call Of Duty, When Duty Calls."
