The Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival officially announced its full 2019 lineup, headlined by Childish Gambino -- who recently wrapped up his final tour under the monicker -- Tame Impala and Ariana Grande.Hip Hop will heavily be represented over the two weekends -- April 12-14 and 19-21 -- in Indio, California.

The expansive lineup includes Kid Cudi, Anderson .Paak, YG, Playboi Carti, Blood Orange, Pusha T, Tierra Whack, Rico Nasty, Juice WRLD, Ella Mai, Wiz Khalifa, Khalid, SOB x RBE, SiR, and Smino.Also performing on the Sunday bill is a new group called Gucci Gang -- initially thought to be a typo on the poster -- that is comprised of Gucci Mane, Lil Pump and Smokepurpp.Passes go on sale Friday, Jan 4 at 11 am.
