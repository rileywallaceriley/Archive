#Premiere: Kaiju The Unconquerable Drops Off Episode 10 Of His Ongoing Series

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

With his long awaited album finally ready to be unleashed, Kaiju — an artist I’ve been following (and championing) for a while now unveils his brand new visual, “CRINGEWORTHY.”

Set in the no so distant year 2020, following a tie between Donald Trump and Kamala Harris in the elections — which forced a mass recount, plunging the nation into chaos — martial law was implemented.

After citizens were forced into crime and stringent gang affiliation as a survival tactic, leading to historically high crime rates (98%), Nightmare Force ran by the Leader CRINGEWORTHY (Kaiju) formed to aid citizens and patrol areas from bandits and mobs.

The incredibly intricate storyline and visual experience is something we’ve come to expect from Kaiju; the some itself is an absolute earworm.

This guy is a crazy good lyricist, which makes his music even more fun as it stands alone, even without the visual accompaniment.

It’s welcomed entry into the series — sitting amongst the top two, easily.
