Muuzictyme Entertainment Network Teams Up With DaBaby To Launch New Concert Streaming Platform

2020-02-25 — news

Muuzictyme Entertainment Network — the world’s premier black-owned, independent pay-per-view concert network — is teaming up with HipHopDX 2019 Rapper of the Year DaBaby to launch their innovative new platform that brings live concert experiences to smartphones and smart TVs across the globe.Alongside a handful of guests (including 42 Dugg, Yo Gotti and more), the “Suge” rapper will be headlining an exclusive pay-per-view concert on March 15 in Detroit, Michigan, hosted by DJ Envy and Angela Yee of Power 105’s The Breakfast Club.For $29.99, fans will be able to stream the show live and enjoy bonus content -- such as the exclusive backstage pass hosted by Ray J and Jackie Long.Alongside promoters Yung Gooch Promotions and New Day Entertainment, Muuzictyme’s ultimate vision is to create live watch parties all over the world, establishing itself as a multi-genre Netflix for live music.The concert, which includes a pre-show red carpet event, takes place on March 15th at 7:00 PM EST and can be ordered via Muuzictyme’s website.
