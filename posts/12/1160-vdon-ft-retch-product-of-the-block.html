VDon ft. RetcH - Product Of The Block

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Today Harlem producer VDon brings you a new visual for "Product of The Block" ft.

RethH. "Product of the Block" is the third video from VDon’s much anticipated debut album "The Opiate" via NEXT Records/Serious Soundz, featuring the hustlers anthem "Cake" ft.

Dave East and the screwed up Houston to Harlem collab "Get Lifted" ft.

A$AP Ant and Trae Tha Truth.

V'Don has been building an impressive production resume for himself the last few years, working with the likes A$AP Rocky, Wiz Khalifa and many more.

He looks to transition to composer with the release of The Opiate.

The 12-track album features appearances by Smoke Dza, Dave East, A$AP Ant, A$AP Nast, Aston Mathews, Retchy P, Da$H, Crystal Caines, Darklo, Willie the Kid and more with VDon at the helm handling all production duties.
