Dave East Blames The Internet For Exaggerating His "Old Town Road" Hate

2019-04-24 — news

During a recent interview with The Zach Sang Show, “Old Town Road” mastermind Lil Nas X (once again) responded to Dave East’s criticism of his viral hit.Although he initially acknowledged the rapper with a tweet on April 18, this time he was more direct, noting “I do not give a fuck about what Dave East is saying.”https://youtube.com/watch?feature=youtu.be&v=cvJz7OJ8p-wAccording to a Wednesday (April 24) Instagram Story, East thinks Lil Nas X responded correctly.“You gonna ask the lil nigga what he thinks about what I say, he ain’t supposed to give a fuck what I say,” East says. “I don’t give a fuck about what nobody says.“I couldn’t even get mad [at him],” he adds, noting that while he saluted him for doing his thing, he still thinks it’s a bad song. But he did blame the internet for blowing it out of proportion.“Internet tried to twist the whole shit,” he says.East took to Instagram last week letting fans know his thoughts on the Billboard smash, stating: “I don’t know what the fuck is going on with Hip Hop; with rap [and] I ain’t no hater but that shit is wack.
