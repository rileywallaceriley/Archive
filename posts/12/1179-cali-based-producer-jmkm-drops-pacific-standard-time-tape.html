We're currently here in the office jamming JMKM -- a Music Producer, Sound Designer, Audio Engineer and Content Creator for Productive Culture.

Originally from Hilo, Hawaii, JMKM is now based in Los Angeles, California; however, he's brought that laid back, carefree, island vibe with him.

His latest beat tape, Pacific Standard Time, is his fourth release -- and first of 2016.

It's a collection of seven, bassy, punchy beats he cooked up from winter 2015 through NYE 2016.

Aside from his beat tapes, JMKM curates a bimonthly producer showcase series called "The Ripple Effect."
