MC Lyte Drops an Inspiring New Video (video)

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

A moment of positive clarity – veteran femcee MC Lyte teams up with 10Beats and Common in the new track Dear John.

The track is described as the anthem for #EducateOURMen, Lyte's scholarship initiative on behalf of her Hip-Hop Sisters Foundation.

This year, the foundation will present dozens of scholarships earmarked to send men to HBCU's.

Russell Simmons, Dr.

Benjamin Chavis, Jada Pinkett Smith and Faith Evans are just of the few celebrities who support the foundation's movement.

It's great to hear some positive voices in the game – helps balance out the garbage and negativity.
