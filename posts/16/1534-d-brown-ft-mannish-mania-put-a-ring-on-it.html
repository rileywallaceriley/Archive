D-Brown ft Mannish Mania - "Put A Ring On It"

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Currently putting the finishing touches on his debut LP Eli Made Me Do It -- slated for a first-quarter release -- Gary, Indiana, rapper D-Brown gives fans a taste of what to expect with the slow and sexy single "Put A Ring On It" featuring the electric r&b duo Mannish Mania.

RELATED: D-Brown Preps His Upcoming LP “Eli Made Me Do It” & Drops New Single

Over the radio-ready instrumental, D-Brown's gruff cadence comes with the straight talk for the ladies and juxtaposes with the smooth as silk melody on the chorus and Mannish Mania's back to back bars.

D-Brown tells AAHH “I’m making trap music with a message and not just mumbling anything behind a trap beat." The quality of this single is an exciting look at what he has in the cannon ready to pop.
