Brianna Perry Releases "Fortune Cookie" EP Featuring Offset, Gunna & More

2018-12-17 — news

After a long winding road that included frustrating label drama, 26-year-old Brianna Perry released her long-awaited Fortune Cookie EP on Friday, December 14 -- featuring appearances by Gunna, Offset, BlocBoy JB, Ball Greezy, and more.“I feel like it’s a lot of flavors, different songs. I can’t even describe it, man.

You just got to listen," Perry told HipHopDX during an interview this past August when asked about the project.“Not having that big machine behind me, I went back to my roots ... I got to grow not only as an artist but as a young woman," she explained. "I just put it in my mind that I wasn’t going let the situation [with the label] stop me from chasing my dream that I had. I feel like somebody who’s followed my journey will see what hard work can get you.
