Blacastan And Apathy Guest On Shifty Eyes' 'Gas And Gods'

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Coming straight out of Alberta, Canada, self-sufficient indie rapper Shifty Eyes is reveling in experiences; from supporting major acts across North America, to frequenting well-known studios, he's building a solid buzz and a stable foundation. "From the struggles of the street to the hustle of the corporate music industry, Shifty Eyes has emerged as an experienced, professional and extremely talented performing/recording artist." Having overcome drug addiction at a younger age, he's put it all into his all into music, and the results are beginning to pay off.

His latest — self-engineered — new mixtape A New Day is a testament to that.

The hard working Emcee and father of three wants to be a "positive role model for his family and — most importantly — to provide inspiration to aspiring musicians.

He's bringing something different and working to stand out in a sea of commonalities.

He was mentored by some of the top producers in the game, while helped him to elevate his engineering skills on this new project — something he's very proud of.

His latest drop from the project is "Gas And Gods" featuring the much hailed Connecticut artist Apathy and Blacastan, who recently dropped Watson & Holmes alongside producer Stu Bangas.
