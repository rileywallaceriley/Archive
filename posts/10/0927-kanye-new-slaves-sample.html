Kanye is, and always has been a perfectionist.

Artists have often remarked that after recording a song with him, they are taken aback by the evolution the song takes behind the scenes as Kanye works his magic.

This was the case for Benjamin Bronfman (part of the Teachers collective), a producer friend of Kanye West, who was contacted by Yeezus and urged to send him some beats for an upcoming project – which culminated as the critically acclaimed Cruel Cold Winter, obviously rubbed Yeezy the right way.

It wasn't until Benjamin attended a listening party that he realized that his track had been used – and how Kanye had evolved (or rather devolved) the sound.
