Roddy Ricch Arrested On Felony Domestic Violence Charges

2019-08-14 — news

It appears that 20-year-old Compton MC Rodrick Moore — better known as Roddy Ricch — is in some hot water.

According to TMZ, The “Die Young” rapper was arrested on Sunday (August 11) after allegedly grabbing his girlfriend during a heated argument.https://youtu.be/HyeZIFwQFNsThe incident allegedly took place early in the morning of August 11 at Ricch’s apartment in Los Angeles.

After the situation turned physical, police arrived on the scene and booked him on felony domestic violence.

He was later released on $50,000 bond.His girlfriend didn’t sustain any injuries that required medical attention.The situation is a low in what has been a year of highs for Ricch.

He most recently appeared alongside ScHoolboy Q, Quavo and A$AP Ferg on E-40’s “Chase The Money” single.
