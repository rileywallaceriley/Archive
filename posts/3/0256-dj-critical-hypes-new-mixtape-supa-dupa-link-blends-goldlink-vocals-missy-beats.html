DJ Critical Hype’s New Mixtape “Supa Dupa Link” Blends Goldlink Vocals & Missy Beats

2019-08-25 — news

DJ Critical Hype, who gained widespread notoriety with blend tape projects The Damn.

Chronic and the follow-up More 9th, is back with his latest official mixtape Supa Dupa Link.

The tape mashes up DMV natives Goldlink and Missy Elliott — who holds the top three spots on HipHopDX’s list of 20 best albums from artists who happen to be women.“I think it’s just a dope combo,” the DJ told Audiomack during a recent interview. “I think it will help people see GoldLink in a different light ...

I think showcasing over some of Missy’s big hits with Timbaland production could help introduce him to a different listener.”Though the two have never officially collaborated, Goldlink did sample Elliott’s vocals from “She’s A Bitch” for the song “Spectrum,” which appears on his debut album After That, We Didn’t Talk.https://youtu.be/RPs7qhCXBgUThis release is the follow-up to 2018’s Wu-Chainz — a meticulous blend of 2 Chainz vocals and Wu-Tang Clan instrumental — a tape that was lauded by RZA himself.”Next up for the Vancouver-based DJ is a mash-up of J.
