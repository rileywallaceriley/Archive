Kanye West Files Trademarks For "Yandhi" Merch

2018-09-25 — news

Kanye West's brand power is undeniable. Now, he's banking on the anticipated success of his upcoming album Yandhi, according to documents obtained by The Blast.

Not only can fans look forward to the music, but also (potentially) a whole lot of apparel in the pipeline.'Ye reportedly filed the necessary documents to trademark the name on September 19, 10 days before the album's release.
