#DXCLUSIVE: Shabaam Sahdeeq & J57 Tease Joint LP With "Razor Sharp Vernacular” Single

2019-08-14 — news

Lyricist Lounge alumni Shabaam Sahdeeq and producer J57 are currently putting the finishing touches on their brand new collaborative LP.

In anticipation, the duo decided to release a loose single as a “warning shot,” to let people know what to expect."Razor Sharp Vernacular,” produced by J57, features verses from DMV spitter Born Unique as well as TriState of Durag Dynasty — a trio that also includes Killer Ben and Planet Asia.As Shabaam told HipHopDX in a recent interview, his relationship with J57 goes back a few years.“Me and J57 met at the Fat Beats flagship store ... we always stayed cool.

I went to events there frequently and did business with Fat Beats on some independent records that I put out over the years,” he explained.“We finally got to work together on a song called ‘GMR,’ which I put out as a one-off single … it did very well.”The single blossomed into an EP, and eventually became a full-length LP dropping soon — featuring Planet Asia, Casual, Nems, Rim, TriState, El Da Sensei, J Hood, DV Alias Kryst, Born Unique, Recognize Ali and more.Stream Shabaam Sahdeeq and J57’s "Razor Sharp Vernacular" featuring Born Unique and TriState below."Razor Sharp vernacular" feat.
