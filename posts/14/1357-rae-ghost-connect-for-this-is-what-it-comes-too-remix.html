Rae & Ghost Connect For 'This Is What It Comes Too' Remix

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

“Me and Ghost are an institution and I know the fans were calling for a remix; so this is what it comes to; Ironman Lunch Mix.” --Raekwon
For 25 years, Raekwon has been one of the most sought out voices in music; a true originator of lyricism, street fashion and inventive music.

On 3-24-2017 the legendary emcee will release his seventh solo album, The Wild.

Released through his own IceH20 imprint along with EMPIRE, the album is a stark commentary on both the world and the music industry--everything is wild, Rae notes, alluding to his new album’s symbolic title.

Just when it looked as though his brethran Ghostdini wouldn't make the track list, on the eve of the release of his new classic, Raekwon premiered the remix for “This Is What It Comes Too” with Billboard. “Ghost is my brother and we’ve been on this blessed journey for awhile now” Raekwon commented to Billboard. “Me and Ghost are an institution and I know the fans were calling for a remix; so this is what it comes to; Ironman Lunch Mix.” The remix for “This Is What It Comes Too” f/ Ghostface Killah will be included as a special bonus track on The Wild. https://soundcloud.com/iceh2orecords/this-is-what-it-comes-too-remix-feat-ghostface-killah
