Nef The Pharaoh Releases "The Big Chang Theory" LP

2018-08-10 — news

Fresh off of a high-profile feature on G-Eazy's "Power" and collaborative EP with incarcerated rapper 03 Greedo, California MC Nef The Pharaoh releases his sophomore LP The Big Chang Theory, the 14-song follow-up to his 2017 debut The Chang Project.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V1RaFTfFieUThe LP comes after a two-week rollout that included the release of a visual for "Big Boss Chang," as well as two singles, "86" featuring Cuban Doll and the Dej Loaf-assisted "What We Have."
