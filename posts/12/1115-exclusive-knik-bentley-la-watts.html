(Exclusive) Knik Bentley – LA Watts

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Knik Bentley is bringing a whole new meaning to the musical term 'diverse.' From his smooth yet powerful voice to his catchy hooks, and with his unique use of metaphors – his style truly deserves a one-word description, different!

Knik has been writing and performing music since the age of nine.

He has shared the stage and some songs with many artists throughout the country.

With epigrammatic lyrics and metaphors, you can't help but move to, Knik is pushing the margins of music in many genres .

Check out his latest single, LA Watts – and get familiar!
