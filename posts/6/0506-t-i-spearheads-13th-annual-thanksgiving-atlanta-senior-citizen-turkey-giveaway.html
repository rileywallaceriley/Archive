T.I. Spearheads 13th Annual Thanksgiving Atlanta Senior Citizen Turkey Giveaway

2018-11-23 — news

King of the South T.I. has been in the generous habit of giving away turkeys during Thanksgiving for years now, but this year he added a new twist — full meals.

His 13th annual Harris Community Works Thanksgiving GiveAway took place on November 21, 2018, and focused on giving Atlanta seniors a little extra love for the holidays.Embed from Getty ImagesThough he was in South Africa on business, he rushed home to make it back and spend the holiday with the Senior Citizens of his neighborhood; the same neighborhood that made him who he is today.“We been there... filling the gap in our community...

Doing our part for the people 13 years straight,” he wrote via Instagram. “It’s been an absolute pleasure.

Always honored to do what we can for the community that was there for me first.“Tip was joined by numerous community leaders, including Mayor Keisha Lance Bottoms, Councilwoman Andrea Boone, and Fire Chief Slaughter.
