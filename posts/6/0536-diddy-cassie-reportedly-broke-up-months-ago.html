Diddy & Cassie Reportedly Broke Up Months Ago

2018-10-17 — news

According to a new report, 11 years after first being linked in the media, and six years after making it public, mogul Sean “Diddy” Combs and Cassie are no longer together — and haven’t been for months.A rep for Cassie confirmed the split to website LoveBScott, who also recently revealed that Diddy had been seeing 26-year-old model Jocelyn Chew — which was reportedly the final straw.

Diddy and Cassie previously split and reconciled in 2015 after the Bad Boy Records founder (and father of six) was reportedly linked to another woman Gina Huynh — whom he’s actually been pictured with as recently as this summer.A source told People Magazine that the two remain friends, and that Cassie is going to focus on her music and acting.Diddy meanwhile remains busy (as usual).

Earlier today he teased a production meeting with Teddy Riley, Travis Scott, and Pharrell.
