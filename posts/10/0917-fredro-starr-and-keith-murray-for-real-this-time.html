Fredro Starr and Keith Murray – For REAL

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

You may recall last month's disappointing last minute (literally) cancelation of the epic battle scheduled between Def Squad's Keith Murray and Onyx frontman Fredro Starr.

Well, according to a Tweet from MC War, the promoters who took an online lashing over the flop show, the battle has been rescheduled to November 21. They also informed us that unlike last time it would in fact be available on Pay-Per-View for those of us who aren't planning a trip to ATL to catch Fredro Starr and Keith Murray in the flesh.

You will have to sit through a battle between Bone Crusher and Mike Jones, which honestly sounds terrible – who even came up with that?
