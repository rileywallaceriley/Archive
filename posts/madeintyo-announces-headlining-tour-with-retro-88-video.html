MadeinTYO Announces Headlining Tour With “Retro 88” Video

2019-01-10 — news

MadeinTYO has unleashed an engaging new visual for the song "Retro 88,” which comes from his debut album Sincerely, Tokyo. It includes edits by LoneWolf, whose unique animated style has been seen in videos for Maxo Kream, Wifisfuneral and Nyck Caution.
