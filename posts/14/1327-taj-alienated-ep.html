Taj--Alienated EP

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

“I def owe my love for music to my brother,” says 25-year-old Fayetteville rapper Taj (real name no gimmicks). "As a little boy, he used to ride me around and play instrumentals.

He began recording myself at 13 and --after a hiatus--released his first project titled Young OG Story under the stage name 365 Montana. "Young OG Story showed me that it's possible for me to chase my dreams; that put the battery in my back to go hard to accomplish all of my goals." Now renamed Taj--and with his new brand Est. 2091 brand-- his new project Alienated is full of jazzy hip-hop, bars heaping with progression, and a whole lot of heat.
