Mike Will Made-It, Swae Lee & Young Thug Perform “Creed II” Single “Fate” In A Boxing Ring

2018-11-29 — news

Super producer Mike Will Made-It teamed up with Swae Lee and Young Thug to brought the spirit of the new film Creed II to The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon with a lively performance of “Fate” on Tuesday, November 27.Rocking from inside of a boxing ring, with Mike Will at the center, Swae and Thugger ripped through the standout single. Adding to the theatrics, Swae Lee yelled, “Knockout” as the performance came to an end with towels were thrown.The song appears on the soundtrack of the film, which grossed $55.8 million over Thanksgiving Weekend — the largest holiday opening ever for a live-action film.Mike WiLL himself curated the soundtrack, which features Kendrick Lamar, J.
