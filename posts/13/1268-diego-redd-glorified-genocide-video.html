Hip-hop recording artist Diego Redd returns with the first visual from his new album, "Glorified Genocide." What he describes as a "breaking down the glorification of our devastation over a soulful beat," title track from the former DefJam recording artist delivers a bar heavy, thought-provoking look at the realities of life as an African American in today's society.

The high quality visual itself is depicts the rapper in the back of a cop car, and being involved in an altercation that's become common ground in today's media; eventually, though, the tables turn, with the officer on his knees with his hands up.

Throughout his career, he's had a co-sign from super star Kanye West and has worked with West Coast legends like E-40, WC (Westside Connection), and more.
