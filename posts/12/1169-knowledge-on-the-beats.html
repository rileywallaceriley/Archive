Knowledge On The Beats

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

It all starts with the producer – and this week we're featuring Tranquil Records own Knowledge.

Straight out of White Plains, New York – and representing 914 – he has close to 15 years in the game.

Initially inspired by a host of local artists in his city, he credits Havoc and Alchemist as his production muses; he produces grimy, underground street records with a 90s asthetic blended with a modern trap style.

What's his ear for artists like?

Well, he says his current fave artists locals that include Black Water, Porto Rock, DakingofPanagea Ashanti, and Nile Precise.

Knowledge currently has a stack of beats available for both lease and exclusive purchase on his website.

So if you're looking for some new knock, don't be shy – hit him up!
