Myles Anthony Teams Up With Pusha T For A New Single

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

"O's like I'm selling cheerios for a living." -- Pusha T
Vancouver, British Columbia, resident  and producer Myles Anthony, and his frequent collaborator, the well-travelled/toured, African-born, philanthropic musician -- and now BC resident -- Ezra Kwizera, are back with another super dope single.

Building on their last piece, "Shinin'" is an atmospheric, moody song that you may imagine as a backdrop to a slow-motion training montage for the Rio Olympics, or a Nike commercial.

This time, though, they have a guest -- the G.O.O.D Music head honcho King Push, more commonly known as Pusha T. Push has been making a lot of noise thus far in 2016, with Darkest Before Dawn: The Prelude picking up steam.

He shines with his trademark dealer-bars: "BET, wanna see how I'm living?

Well, come on in we ain't gotta leave the kitchen." The feature is a good look for the BC producer, and an exciting insight into what he has coming up.

He's been busy in the studio preparing a full-length that will feature a multitude of artists over his beats.

If "Shinin'" is any indication of what he has in the stash, the project could prove to be quite successful for the Canadian-native.

Stay tuned! https://soundcloud.com/mylesanthony/shinin-feat-pusha-t-and-ezra-kwizera
