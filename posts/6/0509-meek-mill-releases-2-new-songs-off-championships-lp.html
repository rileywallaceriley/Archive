Meek Mill Releases 2 New Songs Off “Championships” LP

2018-11-22 — news

Fans of Meek Mill are in for a treat this thanksgiving as the Philly-native dropped off two new songs in advance of Championships, his first full-length project since being released from his highly publicized prison stint."Uptown Vibes" features both Fabolous and early Latin trap pioneer Anuel AA — produced by the first official Dreamchaser, Papamitrou.

The second track, "Oodles O' Noodles Babies," is a soulful reminiscing record produced by Dolla Bill Kidz and Kendxll.In advance of the November 30 release, which is also set to feature Cardi B, Meek announced a partnership with Footlocker, allowing fans to now purchase a collector’s booklet for $3.50, which will give them access to the album as well as six months of Tidal streaming.
