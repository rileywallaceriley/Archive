Snoh Aalegra Inks Deal With JAY-Z's Roc Nation

2020-03-09 — news

Snoh Aalegra has joined the Roc Nation family. Fresh off making her NPR Tiny Desk Concert debut and releasing her “Whoa” remix with Pharrell, the Iranian-Swedish singer and No I.D.'s ARTium Recordings have agreed to partnership deal with JAY-Z's label.The announcement comes hot on the heels of her hit single “I Want You Around” overtaking the likes of Chris Brown, Drake, Khalid and Wale to nab the No. spot on the Urban Adult Contemporary radio chart, according to Mediabase.https://youtu.be/4976Fgvf5PsSnoh's chart-topping song is a standout from her sophomore album Ugh, Those Feels Again, which was released on ARTium last August.

The follow-up to her acclaimed debut Feels featured production from a host of producers including Drake beatsmith Maneesh and No I.D. himself.[related_article id="53982" type="news"]Fans can catch Snoh on her Ugh, Those Feels Again Tour, which kicks off in Vancouver, British Columbia on March 18.
