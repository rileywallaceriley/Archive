Absolute Doubt Drops Cat Soup Produced “Pack” Single

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Bubbling up and coming artist Absolute Doubt links up with producer Cat Soup for the new single “Pack.” The melodic vibe forces your head to nod from start to finish — something we’re coming to expect from him. https://soundcloud.com/cobra-tee-zee/sets/absolute-doubt-fucked-up-at His EP When Nightmares Become Dreams features a number of collaborators, such as Lucki, Jay Storm, and Downtime.

His latest release, the six-track Fucked Up At Midnight, dropped August 11 — and is the warm-up to a mixtape scheduled to be released later this year.

For now, stream Absolute Doubt’s “Pack.” https://soundcloud.com/cobra-tee-zee/pack-prod-cat-soup
