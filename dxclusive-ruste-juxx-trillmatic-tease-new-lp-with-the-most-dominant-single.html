#DXCLUSIVE: Ruste Juxx & Trillmatic Tease New LP With "The Most Dominant" Single & Video

2019-10-08 — news

Crown Heights, Brooklyn, rapper Ruste Juxx is going out with a bang this 4th quarter. Prominently, his LP executively produced by Trillmatic, The Most Dominant, is set to drop this October.The Most Dominant (which was initially titled The Bar-Barian) features beats from the likes of Apollo Brown, Snowgoons, Beatminerz, Mephux and more.

As well, 16 bar verses from lyrical heavyweights like Skyzoo and Conway The Machine.In anticipation of the release, Ruste and Trill have released a visual for the title track, filmed by Myster DL of ILL Mannered Films."It's always a pleasure working with Myster DL," says Ruste. "He found a dope location, and we knocked it ... [he put] incredible visuals to my lyrical masterpiece."Trillmatic and DL had already hooked up earlier this year. DL filmed the visual for "Draco" off Trillmatic's EP with Conway, Organized Grime (which he has revealed to HipHopDX will get a sequel).Ruste Juxx fans are in for a busy Autumn.

His collaborative LP with ILL, 12 Rounds In The Clip just dropped in September and a collaborative project with Tone Spliff titled Magma is dropping October 25. That's in addition to this project with Trill (and a few other projects he's been actively promoting).Watch Ruste Juxx and Trillmatic's "The Most Dominant" video above.[apple_news_ad type="standard”]
