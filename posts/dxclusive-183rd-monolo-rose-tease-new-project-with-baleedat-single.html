#DXCLUSIVE: 183rd & Manolo Rose Tease New Project With ”BaleeDat” Single

2019-04-30 — news

Producer 183rd is continuing an already fruitful 2019 — which has thus far included Uptown, a collaborative EP with Smoke DZA and Vado, and Energy, an Ep with Nyemiah Supreme. This time, he’s connected with New York City rapper Manolo Rose for Spring Time Colors Pastells, a project he tells HipHopDX is the first of many.”Me and Manolo Rose been working for a long time and we been plotting this project for about 2 months,” 183rd says of the project, which is the first full body of work they’ve released. ”he time was right the season was perfect.”In advance of the May 3 release, which sports cover artwork inspired by the classic debut album by Ghostface Killah, the duo is unleashing the first single from the project, “BaleeDat,” which sports an unmistakably New York City vibe.
