Kendrick Lamar & Drake To Compete For Top Artist At 2018 ​Billboard Music Awards

2018-04-17 — news

Billboard has released the full list of nominations for the 2018 Billboard Music Awards, which air on NBC on May 20.

Kendrick Lamar leads the way for Hip Hop artists with 15 BBMA nominations, including Top Artist.

K. Dot will vie for this honor against Drake and Bruno Mars, among others.Cardi B is up for eight awards, including the Billboard Chart Achievement Award.

She'll also face off against 21 Savage and Kodak Black for Top New Artist.Drizzy and Lamar are competing against Post Malone, Lil Uzi Vert and Migos for Top Rap Artist.
