NWA For A New Generation

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Have you taken a look at the Billboard charts this week?

If not, don't feel bad, most people usually don't!

Well, if you did, you may have noticed some anomalies – namely the extremely high number of copies of Eazy-E, Ice Cube and Dr.Dre albums that have been moving off of (virtual) shelves since the release of the record-breaking biopic Straight Outta Compton.

Interesting, no one is rushing out to cop any Mc Ren solo joints, but I'll leave that alone.

How cool is that, though?
