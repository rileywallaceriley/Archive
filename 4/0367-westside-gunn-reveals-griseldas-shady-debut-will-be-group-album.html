Westside Gunn Reveals Griselda's Shady Debut Will Be Group Album

2019-04-11 — news

Westside Gunn and Conway The Machine inked a deal with Shady Records in 2017.

Now, it appears fans are finally going to get a Shady release from the Griselda Records crew.On Wednesday (April 10), Westside announced the Griselda family would complete their first official project for Shady within 48 hours."Breaking NEWS!!!!!!" he wrote on Instagram. "This wat EVERYBODY been waiting on the first official GRISELDA x SHADY Project WSG x CONWAY x BENNY x DARINGER BEAT BUTCHA “WWCD” and it’ll be done in the next 48hrs and just know this shit is fucking NOBODY can fuck wit US when we come together this what the Culture Needs....

I’ll take over from here Folks."Westside has spoken about his Shady debut in the past and confirmed Conway’s Shady solo LP was 90 percent complete last summer.

But, it appears the Griselda crew's first major label effort will be a family affair.The Griselda project announcement comes on the heels of Conway releasing the third installment of his Everybody Is F.O.O.D. series.
