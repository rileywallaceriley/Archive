#DXCLUSIVE: Watch JAG’s “Out The Box” Video

2018-10-29 — news

With his latest LP 2700 out in the world and a busy 4th quarter ahead if him, JAG is celebrating with the release of a brand new visual for the booming single ”Out The Box.” The song is produced by Honorable C.N.O.T.E, who has crafted massive hits like Trippie Redd’s "Dark Knight Dummo.”"C.N.O.T.E sent me a pack one day and as soon as I heard this shit come in, I knew it was big,” JAG tells HipHopDX of the song’s inception. “I actually cut it in like an hour tops and sent it back to him the same day.”He adds, “I knew I was going to be in Atlanta for A3C so it was only right to put him in the video, he a legend for real.”Next up for JAG is the West Coast leg of IDK's tour early November, Rolling Loud on December 14 and a 2700 follow-up dropping early 2019.

He also revealed two new songs will be dropping November 9 — one of which is produced by Toronto producer Jordon Manswell (who worked on Daniel Caesar’s breakout LP).Check out the video for JAG’s “Out The Box” above.
