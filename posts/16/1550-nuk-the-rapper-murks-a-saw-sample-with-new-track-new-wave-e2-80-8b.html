NUK The Rapper Murks A "SAW" Sample With New Track "New Wave" ​

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

NUK The Rapper is back with the follow up to the banger “Up a Deuce” producer by The WATCHM.E.N with yet another strong single, "New Wave," also produced the famed production team -- and they really went in with this unique sampling of the horror film SAW.

Delivered with a slick video treatment that captures the darkness of the beat, NUK really has something dope on his hands.

Brother of rapper ReUp Ty, the westside Chicago upstart is more than making his presensce felt; check out the video above, and keep him on your radar. ​​
