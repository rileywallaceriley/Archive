(Video) Ride Along With Koncept & J57 In "The Excitement"

Sun, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Fuck it, we're biased!

There, I said it.

When we like an artist here at AAHIPHOP, we hold them down to the fullest.

That's something we're going to continue to do for the homie Koncept – rapper, closet DJ, and Fat Beats alumni.

He just dropped a super fun new visual off of the must-listen collaboration project with J57, The Fuel EP.

It originally premiered on Fader earlier this week – and we were stoked when he reached out to us with it. The Excitement – featuring Andrew Thomas Reid – is a drunken ride along that takes us everywhere from cabs to Fader's office.

It was shot entirely by the guys themselves around New York City using Go-Pro cameras, and was edited by fellow Brooklyn-ite, director Jawney Quest.

If you haven't already, stream the whole EP below. The Flight LP is dropping in 2016, so stay tuned for that.
