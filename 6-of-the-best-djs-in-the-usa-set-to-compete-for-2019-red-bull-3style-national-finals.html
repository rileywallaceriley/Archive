6 Of The Best DJs In The USA Set To Compete For 2019 Red Bull 3Style National Finals

2019-10-24 — news

Now in its 10th year, the Red Bull 3Style — the largest global DJ competition in the world — officially announced the lineup for its 2019 USA National Finals on Thursday (October 24).DJ Lazyboy, Matsu, Dynamix, DJ Fingersmith, Chris Villa, Shane Canfield will compete to decide who will represent the United States at the 2020 Red Bull 3Style World DJ Championships in Moscow, Russia. This year’s esteemed judging panel includes the legendary DJ Jazzy Jeff, Four Color Zack, 2016 3Style World winner DJ Puffy and this year’s champ, J Espinosa. “I’m thrilled to have had the opportunity to work with Red Bull 3Style as a World Champion for the past year,” Espinosa says.
