Snoop Dogg & Friends Live At The Coachella Valley Wind Up

Sun, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Are you already making plans to visit the 2016 Coachella Music Festival in Coachella Valley?

Well, you'll want to plan to hit up the Coachella Valley Wind Up at the Saxony Convention Centre.

It's a three-weekend party featuring the iconic west coast rapper Snoop Doggy Dogg.

The event, which has two shows slated, is dubbed 'Snoop Dogg & Friends', which is exciting because Snoop has lots of friends, and the possibilities for impromptu appearances are vast.

Over the past 22 years since Doggystyle dropped, Uncle Snoop has released 12 solo records and a host of collaborative projects, and – thanks to putting his stamp on every medium possible – has become a cultural institution.

From Death Row records to No Limit Records, to his work with Pharrell and his reggae album, he transcends genres; with over 35 million albums sold, he's easily one of the most recognizable and revered artists/personalities of our generation. https://youtu.be/_FE194VN6c4 Don't miss your chance to catch Snoop and a bevy of special guests April 14th and April 28th.

General admission and VIP tickets are available now.

Added cool factor: he's going to be filming a live video at the event.

In fact, if you're a model and are interested in auditioning, you'll want to stay tuned to the Coachella Valley Wind Up website for more info. &nbsp;&nbsp;
