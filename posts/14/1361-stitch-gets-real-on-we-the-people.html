Stitch Gets Real On 'We, The People'

Sun, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

STITCH81CLASSIC—formally known as Sorceress—is not a newbie in the music industry.

Coming out of Kansas City, Missouri, she's known for her unusual rhythmic patterns, deep timbre, charismatic energy, and highly rich content.

Sonically, it's reminiscent of the 90s underground hip-hop movement that included acts like Company Flow; lyrically advanced, without a doubt.

Having performed as a supporting act for names like Slick Rick and Kevin Gates, and having worked with charted producers, she has a track record of putting in work.

Her latest, “We, The People,” is now available on all online digital distribution channels like iTunes, Tidal, Google Play, YouTube, and Spotify.

She's currently wrapping up her latest Ep titled Short Stories, due to release late 2017, so stay tuned.
