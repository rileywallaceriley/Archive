#DXCLUSIVE: Lavish The MDK Drops New Single “Do That Shit Again”

2019-05-20 — news

Rapper Lavish The MDK christens his partnership with Foundation Media with a Schife and Ben Billion$ produced single "Do That Shit Again.”According to Lavish, the loosie — a follow-up to December's ”So What” single — is just a taste of what’s to come.https://youtu.be/5rWpAkz2xXg“Me, LunchMoney, Ben Billion$, and Schife were cooking up late night in the studio like we always do, and as soon as I heard the beat I got a vibe,” Lav tells HipHopDX.He adds, ”Lil Pahokee' The Mixtape coming soon. For now, just enjoy these loosies."It’s been a busy 2019 already for the song’s producers.

Schife, who among other things is a producer working with We The Best, and Ben Billion$ (producer of numerous hits including Yo Gotti’s ”Down In The DM”) most recently both put in work on DJ Khaled’s Father of Asahd.Stream Lavish’s ”Do That Shit Again” above.
