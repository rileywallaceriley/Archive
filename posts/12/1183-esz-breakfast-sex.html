EsZ – Breakfast & Sex

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Today we have a new joint by New York rapper EsZ -- a rapper who's no stranger to the staff here at AAHIPHOP.

In the past, we've featured his songs "Rose Petals" and "Away From Me." His latest is a change of pace from what we've shared with him thus far. "Breakfast & Sex" is fresh, to the point: "if you say you gonna give it girl then give it girl, don't even flex, the BS that I want in life is Breakfast and Sex." Over catchy production, the track is worth a few spins.

Drop it into your weekend playlist!
