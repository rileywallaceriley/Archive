Stalley Ohio Album Stream

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

MMG spit kicker Stalley is blessing his loyal fan base and streaming his debut album, Ohio, today on his Soundcloud page – the album is set to hit store (on and offline) shelves October 27th.

The project features Ty Dolla $ign, Nipsey Hussle, Rick Ross, August Alsina, De La Soul, and Rashad.

It's an incredible look at what's to come!

If you're feeling it, be sure to support Stalley and pre-order the album from I-Tunes.
