Jazz Will – 7 Straight To Heaven

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Following the release of his fiery single "Blackout," Houston-area rap artist Jazz Will has released his new project titled 7 Straight To Heaven.&nbsp;Featuring two collabs, one with DJ Chose and the other with fellow rapper Jono, 9-track self-produced EP fails to disappoint further cementing Jazz Will’s arrival and imprint in the authentic hip hop culture. 7 Straight To Heaven is now available on DatPiff at http://bit.ly/1UcjajL
