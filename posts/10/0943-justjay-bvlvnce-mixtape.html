Born during the 90’s in Mount Vernon, NY, raised in both the Bronx and New Rochelle.

Jesse “JustJay” Scott is an up and coming artist looking to place meaning and purpose back into today’s music, as well as his listeners.

His heavy lyrics and sometimes absurd references mainly attack the media and the propaganda in which they spew upon the public.

Up to date he remains apart of New Rochelle, NY’s elite Hip-Hop collective Inspiration and Innovation a.k.a.

I&I The Tribe.
