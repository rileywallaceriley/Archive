VAS LEON Is Beyond 'Active' Going Into 2017

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

London, UK, singer, rapper, and producer VAS LEON is quick to name Michael Jackson as his biggest musical influence; greats often draw influnce from greats.

He's been building a heavy buzz gigging across the UK, Europe, and the USA and has become a part of the successful Miami-based collective GTPS.

VAS has shared the stage with the likes of DJ Fresh and Ella Eyre--while on tour with Grammy award winning Fat Boy Slim--gaining further exposure and momentum.

Last year, he released his debut LP, Krossover.

Critically acclaimed, it was an outstanding self-produced collection of tracks relating to a universal audience.

It also debuted at no. 14 in the UK Hip Hop charts--impressive, right?

He's takeing his already unconventional approach to rapping to an entirely new level with his upcoming project, Krossover Deluxe.

Truly a storyteller in a class of his own, he is set to take 2017 by storm.
