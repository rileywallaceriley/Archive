Conway The Machine & ScHoolboy Q Eat Alchemist's Production On “Shoot Sideways” Single

2020-03-26 — news

Ahead of the release of their joint project LULU, Griselda’s grimiest Conway The Machine and producer Alchemist (fresh off of The Price Of Tea In China with Boldy James) unleash their ScHoolboy Q featured single “Shoot Sideways.”The Buffalo MC premiered the song with Ebro Darden on Wednesday (March 25), while also sharing that this collaboration is just the first of many.“I love Q,” he told Darden. “We have a few joints together.”When asked by Darden if fans could one day expect a full collaborative project, Conway quickly replied, “I’d love to ... I definitely wouldn’t object to that.”[related_article id="55072" type="news"]He adds, “He just be doing so much, and I’m doing so much that we haven’t been able to get in the studio and lock in for a few days.”Listen to Conway The Machine and ScHoolboy Q’s “Shoot Sideways” produced by Alchemist above. LULU drops March 30.[apple_news_ad type="standard”]
