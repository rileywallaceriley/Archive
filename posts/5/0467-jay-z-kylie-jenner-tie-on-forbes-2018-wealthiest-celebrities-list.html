JAY-Z & Kylie Jenner Tie On Forbes' 2018 Wealthiest Celebrities List

2018-12-19 — news

Forbes annual ranked list of people who hold more wealth than any of us can possibly fathom has been released.

There aren't often fresh faces near the top of the food chain, but this year, 21-year-old new mother Kylie Jenner made her debut at No. 5 with a net worth of approximately $900 million.

What's even more impressive is she tied with none other than JAY-Z.Kylie Cosmetics, a brand Jenner owns 100 percent of that leverages her plugged-in social media following, has done over $630 million in sales since launching just over two years ago.

Jigga was this year's biggest gainer, pulling in $90 more in net worth (over 2017) through his stakes in Armand de Brignac champagne and D'Ussé cognac and shares in both Roc Nation and TIDAL.Diddy came in No. 8 this year, with (a measly) $825 million, and the top 3 include Oprah, Steven Spielberg, and -- not surprisingly -- George Lucas, who is worth over $5.4 billion.You can check out the full ranking, here.
