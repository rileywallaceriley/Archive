Blueface Reportedly Facing 3-Year Prison Stint In Handgun Case

2019-02-20 — news

It appears things have gone from bad to worse for Blueface. Following his arrest earlier this month, L.A.

County D.A.’s office has officially charged the “Thotiana” rapper with felony possession of a loaded weapon. According to The Blast, he's facing up to three years in prison.LAPD arrested Blueface and two members of his entourage in downtown L.A. on February 1 after responding to a report of “rappers in the area.” He was released after posting $35,000 bail.This isn’t the only legal trouble he’s currently facing.

The burgeoning artist is due in court this week after being arrested on a $50,000 bond for assault with a deadly weapon last November. His charges in that case have since been downgraded to possession of a registered handgun, which could land him a year in prison.https://youtu.be/V6EpIABAKKo
