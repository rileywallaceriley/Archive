Burna Boy Releases “African Giant” Featuring Future, YG, Damian Marley & More

2019-07-27 — news

Fresh off his African Giant Tour and hot on the heels of last month’s single “Anybody” — which he performed on Jimmy Kimmel Live! Wednesday (July 24) — Nigerian Afrobeats artist Burna Boy releases his fourth studio LP, African Giant.https://youtu.be/BuMM9vv4Gt0The 19-song project (which also includes the previously released singles “On The Low,” “Gbona,” and “Dangote”) features appearances from Jorja Smith, Zlatan, Jeremih, Serani, M.anifest, Damian Marley, YG and Future.2019 has already been a stellar year for the 28-year-old who, aside from snagging a 2019 BET Award winner for “Best International Act,” appears on the Beyoncé-produced The Lion King: The Gift soundtrack with his track “"JA ARA E." He’s also heading back on the road for his African Giant Returns Tour, which kicks August 9 in Toronto, Canada.Stream Burna Boy’s African Giant below.
