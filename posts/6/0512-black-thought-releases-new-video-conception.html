Black Thought Releases New Video “Conception”

2018-11-19 — news

Following a performance at the fourth annual TIDAL X: Brooklyn benefit concert,  Black Thought has teamed up with the streaming platform again to premiere his new video for “Conception” featuring Reek Ruffin.The track is the first single from his upcoming Streams Of Thought Vol. 2 project.

The follow-up to Vol 1, a collaborative EP with 9th Wonder & The Soul Council that dropped earlier this year, is produced by Nas and Fugees collaborator Salaam Remi.Aside from his new EP, Black Thought is working on a supergroup project with Phonte and Rhymefest.

The busy MC has also recorded upwards of 200 songs for the next Roots studio album.Check out Black Thought’s “Conception” video above.
