Upper West Side (New York City) rapper Katiah One is still reveling in his debut EP A Whole Lotta Water Works, which dropped November 2018.

On a mission of changing the common perception of the average rapper, he draws inspiration from R&B and Rock--and artists like Freddy Mercury, Sam Cooke, and Issac Brock--all of which is evident when dissecting this eclectic body of work. https://www.instagram.com/p/BqaotcwFzOP/ Including "Tail Spin," a single we featured in early December, the four-song project is a statement.

Katiah’s goal with his music is to be a positive influence on the world and make people smile.

Currently affiliated with FiveSe7enCollective, founded by producer and artist J57, he's well on his way to making his mark in the game.
