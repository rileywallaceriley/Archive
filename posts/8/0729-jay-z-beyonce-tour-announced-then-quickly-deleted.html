JAY-Z & Beyoncé Tour Date Announced & Then Quickly Deleted

2018-03-05 — news

Rumors have been swirling around for a while now, suggesting that JAY-Z and Beyoncé were cooking something up together.

On Monday (March 5), fans were given a glimmer of hope about a possible tour, although it was quickly retracted.The opening date of the On the Run 2 Tour -- allegedly set to take place Monday, July 30 in Philadelphia at Lincoln Financial Field -- was listed on both Queen Bey’s Facebook and Ticketmaster.

However, it was soon deleted, but not before fans could notice and collectively freak out.We'll keep you posted once we hear more on what's going on with the possible tour.
