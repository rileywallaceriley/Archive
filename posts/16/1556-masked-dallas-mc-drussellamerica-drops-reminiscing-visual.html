Masked Dallas MC DRussellAmerica Is On The Radar 👀

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

If only comparable to DOOM by sheer virtue of masking his identity, alluding new artist DRussellAmerica is definitely one to keep on the radar.

His $18,000, Jose Fernandez designed mask — Fernandez being a designer for Marvel (hence the aesthetic) — creates this eerie vibe, setting himself as a character, which makes for captivating visual treatment.

Case and point, his latest, “Reminiscing.” https://www.instagram.com/p/BqJX_iiBDVu/?utm_source=ig_share_sheet&igshid=1qs98uzh4h5x6 &nbsp; The song is off of his latest nine-song LP, F*ck Tomorrow," which was released in late 2018 — and created some buzz after being featured on WorldStar Hip Hop.

There is definitely a captivating mysteriousness about him; check out “Reminiscing,” and give the full project a spin, below.
