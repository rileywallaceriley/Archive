Leroy Brown And A Boogie With The Hoodie Collabo Gets A Video Treatment

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

We've been onto Leroy Brown for a while now — and now he's blessing us with a new visual.

The Corona, Queens, Emcee had made some noise over the last year with his collaboration with the much-hyped young rapper A Boogie With The Hoodie, "Let's Get It," which was (purportedly) label suppressed due to friction between the two, has now emerged as a video treatment.

Conceptually it's a melding of a home invasion with dramatic drone footage that screams quality — above all else.

If you're a fan of the track, or if you're just discovering it, you won't be disappointed. https://youtu.be/yU5SWoNydkw
