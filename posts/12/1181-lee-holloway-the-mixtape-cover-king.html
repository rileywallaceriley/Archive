Lee Holloway: The Mixtape Cover King

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

While on the hunt for a digital artist to help him design a mixtape cover, entrepreneur Lee Holloway discovered a huge niche.

Many of the providers that existed in the market either used cheap looking templates or lacked the intimate knowledge to create credible urban artwork, which resulted in low-quality results.

Lee, better known as the Mixtape Cover King, began offering custom, one of a kind cover, and before long things took off, in a major way.

Once he had over 100 orders, he realized he needed to find a way to make this into a business that could help feed his family.

In 2012, Mixtape Cover King officially launched online and has since done notable covers for the likes of the dab-gods, Migos, Mr.

Never Tired, Kevin Gates, Twista, numerous Coast 2 Coast DJ's and much – much – more.

With a commercial on Vh1's signature urban series, Love & Hip-Hop, to international acclaim, Lee and his team clearly have the game on lock; although, their breadth of offerings doesn't stop at mixtapes.

Bottom line, if you're an indie artist, you need to put your best foot forward.
