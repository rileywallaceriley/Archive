Shaq’s Legendary Collab with Biggie Hits DSPs for the First Time

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Shaquille O'Neal and Jersey Legend Productions are reviving the magic of Shaq's under-hailed gem, You Can't Stop The Reign.

This 1996 album, released under O'Neal's TWisM record label, brought together some of the biggest names in hip-hop at the time, including The Notorious B.I.G., Jay-Z, Mobb Deep, and Rakim.

The album also featured appearances from Bobby Brown, DJ Quik, and Ralph Tresvant, among others.

The title track, which celebrated Shaq's dominance on and off the court, as well as B.I.G.'s incredible musical talent, became a Billboard Top 100 hit on the Hip-Hop/R&B Airplays chart and reached No. 17 on the official Dance Singles Chart.

Shaquille O'Neal hinted at the re-release during a recent episode of his podcast,

The "You Can't Stop The Reign" single is now available for the first time on all digital streaming platforms, and the full album is set to officially drop on June 28, 2024.
