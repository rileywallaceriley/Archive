BK's Scion.Rae Delivers A 'Bittersweet' New Track

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

“It's good to hear Hip-Hop like this again.”– SeeSantana
BK don't play; native New Yorker Scion.Rae is taking listeners on a musical journey.

You can take a peek into every aspect of his mind through his music.

What his music lacks in grit, it picks up in soul and grizzly lyricism.

He spins tall tales about anything which crosses his mind--always something new, always something fresh.

The young man may be new to the game, but don’t sleep on the kid.

His newest record "Bittersweet" taps into the flute craze currently locking down the game, and delivers a gorgeous atmospheric joint with crazy wordplay.

Worth a spot on your weekend playlist! https://soundcloud.com/scionrae/bittersweet
