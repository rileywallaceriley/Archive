&nbsp;Nothing speaks louder than experience.

In 2012, Strata was almost killed after being shot in the back by a Tempe Police Officer while running away with a cell phone in his hand.

They arrested him, and he fought his case in Maricopa County Jail for three years before finally being given the chance to bond out on 275k.

Now, with a second chance at life, he's developed his music into his unique sound.

He feels this is the best way to speak out against this epidemic and honor those who weren't as lucky as him.

His haunting and powerful new single/video, #BlackLivesMatter, is his chance to say non-violently enough is enough – the violence needs to stop! https://youtu.be/qHMoksxnTdA You can expect the unexpected from Strata.

Hard lyrics over a soulful beat, or his r&b twist, Club Bangers, EDM, his catalog has no limit.

Every track is REAL RAW AND RELEVANT, and everything the game needs right NOW!
