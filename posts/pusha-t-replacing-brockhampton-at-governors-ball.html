Pusha T Replacing BROCKHAMPTON At Governors Ball

2018-05-29 — news

Following what has been a rough weekend for Hip Hop boy band BROCKHAMPTON, it has now been confirmed that G.O.O.D. Music president Pusha T will be replacing them on an already stacked lineup at the Governors Ball Music Festival in New York this Saturday (June 2).Joining a Saturday schedule headlined by Travis Scott, and featuring sets by 2 Chainz and the ever-illusive Hip Hop unicorn Jay Electronica, Push is serving as the official replacement for the critically acclaimed group following the formal departure of Ameer Vann amid allegations of sexual misconduct.

The group announced the cancellation of the remaining dates on its US tour over the weekend.Eminem, Lil Uzi Vert, Conway The Machine & Westside Gunn, Post Malone, and many more are also scheduled to hit the stage this weekend.[apple_news_ad type="any"]
