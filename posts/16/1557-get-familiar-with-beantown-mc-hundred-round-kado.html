Boston, Massachusettes, MC Hundred Round Kado -- formerly Ricky Gramz -- is preparing to announce his newest White Kargo.

Grinding in the game since 2009, releasing King of the Triple Beam Volume 1, Kokaine Fever, King of the Triple Beam Volume 2 and Marilyn Gunroe under his past monicker, he says his rebrand has allowed him to celebrate his Haitian roots and connect more deeply with both English speaking and French Creole-speaking audiences.

His latest projects Alive At A Wake and Whistling Through The Graveyard are via streaming networks; get familiar now -- and stay tuned for the new LP announcement!
