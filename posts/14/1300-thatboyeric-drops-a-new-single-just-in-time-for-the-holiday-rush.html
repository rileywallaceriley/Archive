ThatBoyEric Drops A New Single—Just In Time For The Holiday Rush

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

18-year-old South-Florida native, ThatBoyEric, is looking to make a significant impact this New Year.

Having released his first mixtape two years ago, and more recently having worked with 'We The Best' recording artist Kent Jones—who enjoyed some heavy Billboard success this year—he's ready to take things up a notch.

His newest single, "I Might," which has already landed some placements around the web, is just a taste of what he has in the vault—ready to go.
