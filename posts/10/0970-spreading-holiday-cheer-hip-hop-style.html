Spreading Holiday Cheer, Hip-Hop Style

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Christmas is a time for feels, love and family – which isn’t exactly the coles notes of your fave rap album for the most part.

R&B, with it’s softer more emotional undertone, has often celebrated the season proudly.

In fact, most of your fave singers likely have a holiday album out now.

There have, however, been some instances of hip-hop celebrating the season the best way it knows how.

We’ve taken the liberty of rounding up some of our top attempts at spreading holiday cheer.

Check out our top 5 hip-hop christmas tunes after the jump!

5: Kanye West Cyhi Da Prynce, Teyana Taylor – Christmas In Harlem
4: Snoop Dogg & The Dogg Pound – Santa Comes To The Ghetto
3: Rev Run ft.

Ma$e, Puff Daddy, Snoop Dogg, Salt N Pepa, Onyx & Keith Murray – Santa Baby
