Neome Alin – Safe To Say

Thu, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

&nbsp;We love bumping into new – and promising – voices here at AAHIPHOP.

One of our latest discoveries is 18 year-old New York City-native Neome Alin, who makes a bold debut with the heart-wrenching single "Safe To Say," an emotional ballad populated with a sparse minimalistic beat and powerful vocals.

Currently signed to NYC label MiddleGround Digital, the song is the first release from her currently untitled EP; with intensely high quality visuals, it's clear that Neome has a great team behind her.&nbsp; With her social following steadily increasing it's clear to see why many people are taking notice of her dynamic vocal range and exquisite sound.

You'll definitely be seeing more of her here on the site.
