Conway The Machine Drops Dave East & Jim Jones-Assisted "Vino D" Single

2019-09-10 — news

As Conway The Machine prepares to release his new album Look What I Became... on Friday (September 13), he's dropped off the project’s second single, “Vino D” featuring Dave East, Jim Jones and Elcamino. “It’s dope to collaborate with two of my homies” the rapper told Hypebeast about working with Jones and East. “They also happen to be two of my favorite rappers.”The JR Swiftz-produced release comes just a week removed from the project’s first single “Tito’s Back” featuring Benny The Butcher and Westside Gunn.“ is a gift to all my day one fans and also my new fans to introduce them to this Griselda shit,” Conway said.https://youtu.be/s9cFnnN4LlELook What I Became… is also set to feature guest appearances and production from Benny The Butcher, Westside Gunn, Dave East, Jim Jones, Alchemist, Statik Selektah, DJ Muggs and Daringer.This, of course, is just an appetizer for his Shady debut God Don’t Make Mistakes, which is expected to arrive this October. 2019 is going out with a bang for fans of the movement, who can also look forward to Westside Gunn’s Hitler Wears Hermes 7 on Halloween and the Griselda group album What Would Chine Gun Do dropping on November 11.Stream Conway’s “Vino D” featuring Dave East, Jim Jones and Elcamino above and tell Shannon Sharpe that Buffalo is solidified.
