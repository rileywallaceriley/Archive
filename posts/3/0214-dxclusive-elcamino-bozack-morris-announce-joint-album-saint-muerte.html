Buffalo MC Elcamino is ending his 2019 with a bang.

In addition to Where's My Pyrex (a collaborative LP with producer Oh Jay), which dropped October 11, and the sequel to his self-titled debut mixtape via Griselda set to release on November 22, he also now has a new LP with Toronto producer Bozack Morris.Announced on Wednesday (October 30), the album titled Saint Muerte features appearances by Benny The Butcher, Rome Streetz and Far Rockaway rhymer Flee Lord -- who appears on the project's first single "Coke Brothers."Morris is the co-founder of GGBR, a Toronto-based boutique label that has done vinyl drops for the majority of Elcamino's catalog, including one earlier this year for Don't Eat The Fruit.

Aside from the digital release of Saint Muerte, Morris will also be opening up the preorders for a limited pressing of Lot & Abraham, which was initially released this past May.

In addition to two bonus colorways, the vinyl also includes instrumentals.Elcamino and Bozack Morris' Saint Muerte officially drops Friday (November 1).
