Keenan Allen Disses Le’Veon Bell New Rap Single: “This Ain’t It”

2019-04-03 — news

Like I said I speak facts.

You shouldn’t have asked if you ain’t want the truth,” he wrote.As an added insult, Allen added: “It should’ve been your real friends that said it tho.”Check out the whole Twitter exchange, below.
