L'S810 Connects With Stik Figa For New Banger

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Michigan-born Kansas MC, soulful Hip Hop artist L'S810 just dropped a brand new single — a remix of his track “Game Right” — featuring Mellow Music Group rhymer Stik Figa.

The record is a off upcoming album Sunny Ave., an album dedicated to the ongoing Flint Water Crisis which began in 2014. Released through his Hip Hop Mastered Productions imprint, the track is a jazzy boom-bap treat, laced with L’s aggressive flow, which is reminiscent of Babe-B-Face Kaos, 1/3 of the Native Tongue affiliated Bush Babees.

Currently working with regional Unlabeled Records, L'S810 can currently be found performing all over the Midwest.

Be sure to check his socials and stay tuned.
