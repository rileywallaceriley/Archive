22-year-old NXUS, who is one-third of the trio known as Dead Reputation (with Delirious Beats and Rake ), continues to impress me.

I was a huge fan of their last collective release, "Rake Cat," and his latest record "Holy wATEr" --and grimy visual-- continues to keep me interested, with an almost Chance The Rapper cadence.

He comes off optically as a blend of Odd Future and A$AP Mobb, by that I mean, he is straight up bars, but he has just the right amount of darkness and mystery to him.

I feel like his music doesn't tell me too much about him, but it's just enough--which I understand is hard to explain.

As I mentioned the last time I wrote about him, I get the vibe from NXUS; he has something about him--you can just feel that he's headed along the right incline.

Give "Holy wATEr," and tell a friend to tell a friend.
