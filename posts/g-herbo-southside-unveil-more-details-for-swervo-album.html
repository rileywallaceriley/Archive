G Herbo & Southside Unveil More Details For “Swervo” Album

2018-07-20 — news

G Herbo and Southside have shared more details around their Swervo collaboration, including the tracklist, cover art and release date.The project, which has been teased for more than a year, has finally been given a release date of July 27.The announcement comes a month after the Chicago rapper and the Atlanta super-producer released a visual for the title track.https://youtu.be/ZSKEihd7VlgProduced entirely by Southside, who’s crafted hits for names like Gucci Mane, Future, Wale, and JAY-Z, the 14-song effort sports features from 21 Savage, Chief Keef, Juice WRLD and Young Thug.The duo also unveiled the artwork, which recalls Eric B. & Rakim's iconic Follow The Leader cover, right down to the custom-made jackets.Check out the cover art and full tracklist below.
