Atmosphere: New Video And Tour Dates

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Fishing Blues is the seventh studio album from Minneapolis hiphop icons Atmosphere. Released this summer, the album has received wide praise, including from Complex who noted, “20 years in, Atmosphere is older, wiser, and still able to make relatable, insightful, and soulful music for their fans.” Last week, the duo released the latest video from Fishing Blues, a powerful visual for track “Seismic Waves.” If you missed it, we sat down for a great conversation with Slug, who told us about how the name of the album came about, staying focused after 20+ years in teh game, and more.

You can catch up on that interview here.

For the real heads, be sure to catch Atmosphere on tour with Brother Ali, deM atlaS, Plain Ole Bill & Last Word.

The first leg of the tour had 15+ sold out shows, so don't be disappointed.

Check out the dates below.
