D-Block MC Whispers Teams With Snyplyfe For "Razor Ramon" Video

2019-10-18 — news

South Bronx native and cousin to rapper Chris Rivers, D-Block MC Whispers, continues to fan the flames of this past summer's Wizardly LP with the release of a brand new video for his single "Razor Ramon" featuring Snyplyfe.The visual is one of two slated to drop in the promotion of the LP, which aside from his cousin and Snyplyfe, also features verses from Sheek Louch and Chris Streetz.
