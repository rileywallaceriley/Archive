We keep getting heat from Jersey's own TwonDon.

Fresh off of the buzzworthy "Black Bar Mitzvah" single, and a standout perforce at the Garden State edition of the much lauded Juice Fest, Twon now unleashes his brand new video "Successful" off of his Paradise Isn't Free EP, which dropped a couple of weeks back.

The video shows Twon from an adolescent to adulthood still battling trials and tribulations to reach success.

With cuts from sipping champagne to snapple, it shows how much our youth can change when enduring new obstacles.

Shot and edited by Vivid Vision Films.
