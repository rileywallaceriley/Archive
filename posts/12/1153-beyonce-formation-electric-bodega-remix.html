Beyonce – Formation (Electric Bodega Remix)

Wed, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

You've seen the video, you saw it live at the SuperBowl and now Beyoncé's 'Formation' gets the remix treatment from the masters, Electric Bodega.

Fresh off of the release of Faded (featuring Dougie F) -- which got major NYC radio love last month -- and their follow-up single, Locked Up, featuring Netousha Monroe, Electric Bodega flips the hottest chick in the game's newest smash into another top-shelf remix.

EB has built an impressive sonic catalogue and earned millions of spins over the last few years, cranking out an endless array of hypnotic remixes for the likes of Future, Chris Brown, Drake, French Montana, Bodega Bamz and many more.

Their work has been featured on respected sites like HYPETRAK, Complex, Dancing Astronaut, Earmilk, as well as, radio stations like Hot 97 and Power 105.

EB's forthcoming project, 24/7 is the first EP of original material, containing features from Dougie F, Nina Sky, Brucie Bee (of the Legendary Rooftop) and Matt Beilis. Stream Beyonce - Formation (Electric Bodega Remix):
