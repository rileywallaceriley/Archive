Philly Artist ISIS Keeps It “Underground” With Her New Single

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Philly has its share is fantastic talent on deck.

Brannew Entertainment just floated us another talented upstart from the city of brotherly love.

ISIS is a singer/rapper with a great look and an even dope style.

Her new single “Underground” has a serious bop, and it’s head-nodding instrumental produced by Respect My Ear compliments her impressive wordplay. https://soundcloud.com/brannew-entertainment/isis-underground-version-1

“You’ll get burned let it hit you in the soul, so you’ll know the kind of pain that the struggle really holds.” —ISIS
Both ISIS and her team represent a shared mindset of people who choose not live a life based on the repetitive ideas of the past, but instead work to improve the world and oneself.

Positivity reigns supreme!

They have one with this record!
