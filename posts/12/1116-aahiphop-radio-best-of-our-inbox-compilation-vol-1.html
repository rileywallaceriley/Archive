AAHIPHOP RADIO: Best Of Our Inbox Compilation Vol. 1

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

We like to think of ourselves as champions of up and coming acts.

In fact, we've been giving many new artists the same artistic and journalistic attention/weighting as major artists here on the site.

We're going to continue that with our new compilation series, Best Of My Inbox.

We're going to be bringing bi-monthly mixtapes showcasing the dopest stuff making it into our inboxes.

As well, we'll be sharing all the necessary links below so you can follow the artists you're feeling the most.

Get on at the ground floor; give the comp a spin and get familiar with what's next.
