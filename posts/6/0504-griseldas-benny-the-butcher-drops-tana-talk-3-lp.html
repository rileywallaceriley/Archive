Griselda’s Benny The Butcher Drops “Tana Talk 3” LP

2018-11-23 — news

After a 2018 full of hype and some stellar guest appearances, Benny The Butcher, 1/3 of the Griselda Records brood celebrates Black Friday and his birthday with the release of his highly anticipated LP Tana Talk 3.

For weeks, the Buffalo MC had been bragging about bringing it back to the bars, and fans definitely won’t be disappointed.The 14-song project features guest vocals from both Westside Gunn and Conway The Machine (obviously) as well as Mayhem Lauren and Royce The 5’9.

As with all Griselda releases, the production has a consistent moody feel — in this case, handled exclusively by in-house beat-smith Daringer, as well as Alchemist.The album will be celebrated along with his birthday tonight in his hometown tonight as part of Westside Gunn’s Machine Gunn Black Friday — an event that WSG claims is worth flying in for.Check out Benny’s Tana Talk 3 down below.https://open.spotify.com/album/5OsHMGOg6lRV9REoVxbcWA?si=UzUSQmTySeO74aIFfvHXYw
