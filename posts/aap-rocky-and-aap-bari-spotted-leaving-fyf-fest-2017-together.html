A$AP Rocky & A$AP Bari Spotted Leaving FYF Fest 2017 Together

2017-07-24 — news

A$AP Rocky and A$AP Bari were spotted leaving Los Angeles' FYF Fest 2017 together in Rocky's white Ferrari over the weekend, suggesting the Mob's starting quarterback, Rocky, is standing behind Bari — the man who initially introduced him to the late A$AP Yams and kickstarted his career.Bari was accused of sexual assault after a disturbing video came to light earlier this month. The A$AP Mob members' reunion comes a week after Rocky possibly took a shot at Bari during his set at the Agenda trade show and concert in Long Beach, California, where he amended the lyrics of "Telephone Calls" to call Bari a "bitch."Since then, there's been no additional public response from the Mob — aside from Playboi Carti's public condemning last week — and Bari has yet to say anything further after his official statement.

It's likely the reconciliation happened offline at some point over the last week.FYF Festival 2017 featured amazing performances from artists like A Tribe Called Quest, Erykah Badu, Missy, and Frank Ocean who brought out actor Brad Pitt.
