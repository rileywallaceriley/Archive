Eminem Celebrates "Relapse's” 10-Year Anniversary With New Merch

2019-05-16 — news

Today marks the 1oth anniversary of Eminem's sixth studio LP, Relapse and fans can celebrate the milestone with some (extremely) limited edition merch."Crack a bottle," Em wrote in the caption of a video posted via Instagram -- referencing the LP's first of four singles, which dropped February 2, 2009.

He then pointed fans to his official website, where they can preorder the 10th-anniversary Relapse RX long sleeve shirt -- but, only for 24 hours.The black shirt, emblazoned with the album title on the right arm, and a prescription on the left breast, will be shipping in five-six weeks, according to the website.Admit It, You Slept On Eminem's RelapseUpon its release, Relapse (the highest selling album of 2009) debuted in the top spot of the Billboard 200 chart, pushing 608,000 copies in its first week -- later earning certified double platinum status in the US.Check out the shirts below.
