Xleondiuz is a Florida-based up and comer we've been following for a minute now--and he's starting the New Year with a bang.

With his new record "100," he's incorporated a Drizzy "Started From The Bottom" vibe --but with more edge to it.

It's an ode to his authenticity and further insight into his reality growing up in the streets of Dade County, one of the top 20 most dangerous cities in America.

It's a great follow-up to "Front Door," which we shared here back in early December.

The record is yet another appetizer to his mixtape Sin Paradise, which he's hoping to drop on us this February.

He also has a new visual for his record "Dade County," which is dropping soon--another record we dropped on you last month.

Check out "100," and if you're feeling it as much as we are double-tap that share button! https://soundcloud.com/leoniduz/100s
