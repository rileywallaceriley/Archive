#DXCLUSIVE: Watch JAG's Visual For Jordon Manswell-Produced "Golden Bird"

2018-11-19 — news

JAG, who recently joined IDK for the West Coast leg of his The Very Bad Run World Tour, just dropped a brand new visual for his loosie single "Golden Bird," produced by Jordon Manswell -- the Toronto-based producer behind Daniel Ceaser's “We Find Love.”The two have collaborated in the past for JAG's 2700 album, on the song "Major," which also Fasodd and LA-based rap/singer Ye Ali (a frequent Manswell collaborator). A visual for that will be dropping in the next week or so, JAG tells HipHopDX.Next up for JAG is Rolling Loud's Friday, December 14, line up -- which is headlined by Post Malone and Lil Wayne -- followed by the 2700 follow-up dropping early 2019.

For now, check out "Golden Bird," above.
