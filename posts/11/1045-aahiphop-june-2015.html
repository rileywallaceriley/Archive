AAHIPHOP June '2015

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Welcome to AAHIPHOP Radio – a pilot initiative here at aboveaveragehiphop.com.

We bring you dope monthly mixes, and give our unsigned submissions a chance to get some shine at the same time.

We’ll be reserving 4-5 slots a month for some of the hottest unsigned submissions we receive here, as well as 1 slot for producers!

So be sure to keep the submissions coming.
