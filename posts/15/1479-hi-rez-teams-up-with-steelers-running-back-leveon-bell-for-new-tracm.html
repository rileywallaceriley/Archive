Hi-Rez Teams Up With Steelers Running Back Le’Veon Bell For New Track

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Pittsburgh Steelers running back Le'Veon Bell AKA 'Juice' and indie juggernaut Hi-Rez, who I’ve covered in the past, have teamed up with Tae Fresh and Dani Devinci for a dope new song, “I Made A Dollar.” Bell (who burns down the last verse) made headlines earlier this year after threatening to sit out the 2018 season if he didn’t receive a franchise tag—which essentially means he’d bank no less than the average of the top five running backs in the league.

Hi-Rez, in case you need a refresher, has one of the most dedicated fan-bases I can think of; his Only For The Fans series has been making some serious noise, and he’s been packing shows across the nation, and even across the border in my city (Toronto). https://twitter.com/hireztherapper/status/959508944136781824 He even made a Fortnite rap; if you’re a gamer, you’re welcome. https://twitter.com/hireztherapper/status/957030665123278848 The new joint is worth a spin—add it to your playlist ASAP.
