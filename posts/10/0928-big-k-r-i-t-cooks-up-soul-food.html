Big K.R.I.T Cooks Up Soul Food

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

With just about two weeks until Def Jam artist Big K.R.I.T drops his highly anticipated third album Cadillactica, he's decided to bless us with a third release from the project. Soul Food, which features Raphael Saadiq, is just as hot as Pay Attention and the title track Cadillactica, which were already leaked – and so far listeners and bloggers are anxious for the full-length.

Check the track after the jump. If you're feeling the track, it can be yours for free with a pre-order of the album on I-Tunes.
