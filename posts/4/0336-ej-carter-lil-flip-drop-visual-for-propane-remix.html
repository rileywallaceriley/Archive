Lil Flip & EJ Carter Release Video For “Propane (Remix)”

2019-05-17 — news

Following the release of their joint project, H Town To Pi Town, rapper EJ Carter and Houston legend Lil Flip premiered their video for the standout single, “Propane (Remix).”The track was the first collaboration between the two MCs; Flip described his first reaction to the song during an interview with HipHopDX.“The song ‘Propane’ was sent to me by someone in my circle about a year ago, and I immediately felt like the world needed to hear it … the world needed to hear him,” Flip says.Carter and Flip are currently on a tour through to June 27 — with the final show taking place in Houston, Texas.Check out the “Propane (Remix)” above.
