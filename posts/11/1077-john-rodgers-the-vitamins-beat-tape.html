John Rodgers – The Vitamins Beat Tape

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Cincinnati, Ohio, based producer John Rodgers present his latest instrumental showcase The Vitamin Beat Tape.

Packed with 13 heat rocks for you to move your pen – and nod your head – to, it’s a diverse offering, showing off his versatility and creativity.

You can download the project in all it's glory, and also purchase production work from John, on his website Once-A-Day-Beats.com.
