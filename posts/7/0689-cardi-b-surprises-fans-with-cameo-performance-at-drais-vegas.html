Cardi B Surprises Fans With Cameo Performance At Drai's Vegas

2018-04-28 — news

Last night (April 27), mom-to-be Cardi B joined fiancé Offset — and the Migos — for an impromptu performance at Las Vegas hot spot Drai’s Nightclub, much to the surprise of the sold-out crowd.Also joined on stage by Denver Broncos defensive end Von Miller and Sacramento Kings center Willie Cauley-Stein, Cardi took the audience by complete surprise, grabbing a mic and erupting on stage to perform her verse for “MotorSport.” Afterward, she and the Migos partied the night away in stageside VIP.Next stop for Cardi and Migos is DC, where they — along with Miguel — are headlining the 2018 Broccoli City Festival.

The show, taking place today, will be Cardi’s final performance until September, when she joins Bruno Mars for his 24K Tour.Cardi may have announced that she would be canceling all her summer dates, but she was quick to reassure fans that she’s still be putting in work.
