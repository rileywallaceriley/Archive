Migos' "APESHIT" Reference Track Reveals Role In Writing Beyoncé's Verses

2018-06-18 — news

Once JAY-Z and Beyoncé dropped their surprise album EVERYTHING IS LOVE project on TIDAL, many fans deduced that Migos were behind the adlibs on "APESHIT." The full album credits proved this to be correct, revealing both Quavo and Offset were actually credited as writers.However, it appears the Migos members were behind more than just random adlibs.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LJDXGT8IFTUMemes pointing out that Queen Bey sounded like a fourth Migos member appear to have more validity than expected.
