DJ Premier Mini-Mix

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

We were saddened to hear about the closure of the legendary D&D studios, which was the creative lair of the iconic producer DJ Premier.

Countless (seminal) albums were recorded there.

So on that train-of-thought, we though we'd start the week off right – with a dope mix of some DJ Premier classics (which were recorded at D&D).

Enjoy, and happy Monday!
