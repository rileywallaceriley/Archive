#DXCLUSIVE: Koncept Celebrates Successes In Opulent “Champagne” Visual

2019-06-21 — news

Following up the release of his Royce Da 5’9 featured ”Watch The Sky Fall 2,” rapper Koncept releases a brand new single/video from his upcoming Champagne Konny due later this summer.”Champagne” produced by Bradley "Hugh" Denniston, is given a larger than life treatment that loosely nods to the lifestyle of The Great Gatsby’s protagonist Jay Gatsby. In an opulent mansion, surrounded by beautiful women — and rocking a fur coat — Kon toasts to success.“[The song] is both a celebration and the gratefulness of being here right now despite all the obstacles, errors, and challenges,” he tells HipHopDX.It’s been an eventful week for the native-New Yorker who announced his upcoming UK tour on Monday (June 17), which includes dates in Manchester, Birmingham, and London.Check out Koncept’s ”Champagne” video above.
