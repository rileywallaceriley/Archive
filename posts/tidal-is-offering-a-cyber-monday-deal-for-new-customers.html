Tidal Is Offering A Cyber Monday Deal For New Customers

2018-11-26 — news

It’s no secret that Tidal has been running the streaming network pack in terms of exclusive content in 2018. From video premieres to events such as their benefit concert that streamed live from Brooklyn, they’ve been ruling timelines this year.Have you been missing out?

If money has held you back from joining the platform thus far, Tidal has extended an offer this Cyber Monday that will alleviate all your excuses.https://tidal.com/video/94907587Until November 27th at 3:00am EST, new clients can grab an offer that will get them $.99 for three months of Tidal premium or — even better — $1.99 for 3 months of Tidal HiFi.
