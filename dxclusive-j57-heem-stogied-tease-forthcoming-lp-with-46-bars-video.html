#DXCLUSIVE: J57 & Heem Stogied Tease Forthcoming LP With “46 Bars” Video

2019-07-19 — news

Producer J57 — who is having a busy 2019 thus far — linked up with ATLien Heem Stogied (close collaborator of Tha God Fahim) for a collaborative LP titled False Guidance. In anticipation of the release, they’ve released a visual treatment for the lead single, “46 Bars.”“It’s my 13th original project ... J57 fully produced it except for two co-productions by Deejay Element (Brown Bag All-Stars) and DJ Pocket,” Heem Stogied tells HipHopDX.

The LP, also features J-Scienide, Ill Conscious, and more.“There will also be three different vinyls for this one album released via Copenhagen Records,” he adds.False Guidance is just one of the various collaborative releases on the docket for J57. In addition to the upcoming Jamo Gang album -- and the others he’s hinted at -- there is an LP with Shabaam Sahdeeq on the way.Check out Heem Stogied and J57’s “46 Bars” above.
