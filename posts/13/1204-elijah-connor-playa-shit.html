Talent runs in the family; Singer/reality star Elijah Connor, the younger cousin of the iconic singer/songwriter Prince -- who tragically passed away last month -- is a great example.

Take one listen to his single, "Playa Shit," and you'll understand why the song is quickly becoming a becoming a favorite on Top 40 and Urban radio stations around the country.

Hailing from Motor City (Detroit), Elijah has made quite a bit of noise as of late -- he's the star of the Oxygen/NBC Series "Player Gets Played," which is wildly successful with over 1.2 million viewers.

He also been featured in commercials, and even apprared on movie screens -- you may have seen him without realizing it.

He's rocked stages with the likes of Diddy, iLoveMakonnen, Barry Manilow, A$AP Rocky, Mario Winans, Rich Homie Quan, Robin Thick, Lil' Kim, Teddy Riley, and Jordin Sparks .

Also, he has been featured in New York Times, VIBE, Seventeen Magazine, and more.

I mean, look, he took vocal lessons from Aretha Franklin!

This kid has been groomed for greatness -- so it feels inevitable.
