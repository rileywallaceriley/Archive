FDA Joins Scarface On His Icon Tour

Sun, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Overgrind Records head honcho, Texas-based rapper FDAmusic, has continued to do big things since we last featured him here on AAHIPHOP.

He's currently back in Texas after hitting the road with OG hip-hop icon Scarface, who is still riding the wave of his Deeply Rooted record, on his Icon tour.

The tour touched down in cities like Tucson, LA, San Diego, and Denver; FDA had a chance to spread further his brand, blessing audiences with some of his biggest tunes, including Hustle, Roley Dreams & Fame, Real One, and his Kevin Gates assisted I Know It.

Of course, the cameras were rolling.

FDA took us along for the ride – check out his on the road videos below.

Also, take an ear to his most recent releases, Bernie Sanders, and Flightman, a reworking of Drake & Future's Jumpman, where he proclaims: "two-hundred thousand singles sold plus them albums."
