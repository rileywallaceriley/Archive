EsZ – Away From Me

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Lyrical visual artist EsZ is back with another new song.

Following up his super dope drops like&nbsp;Rose Petals, Mia Nia, Maybe I'm Trippin', and UpInTheClouds, he's released a new joint entitled Away From Me.

Over a bouncy beat loaded with piano chops, EsZ attempts to expel all negative energy from his circumference.

EsZ, who we've featured in the past, gets better and better each track he drops, and Away From Me is no different.

With bars on deck, and flow that feels fresh and current, EsZ is on the right path – keep your eyes and ears on him.
