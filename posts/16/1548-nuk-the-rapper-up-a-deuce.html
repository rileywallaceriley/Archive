NUK The Rapper - “Up a Deuce”

Mon, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Brother of rapper ReUp Ty, who we’ve been following this year, NUK The Rapper is on the come up — literally — with his brand new banger “Up a Deuce” producer by illustrious production team The WATCHM.E.N. Drenched in a drill sound characteristic of the Windy City and packed with gritty bars engulphed in realness, the Westside Chicago upstart makes his presence felt with this flame emoji offering.

Having honed his style from a young age, NUK has a energetic aura that really stands out — with a sense of all or nothing with every single syllable.
