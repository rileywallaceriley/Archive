Mondays are universally dreaded by many; but Atlanta rapper Tre Cartel is switching that up, by giving you something to look forward to.

Fresh off of his newest EP, Real M.C., which we reviewed last week, Tre is dropping his latest joint Karma.

The slow tempo, bassy track is a stark reminder from young Tre that what you do today, can definitely bite you in the ass tomorrow.

The song is a kick-off to #MM, an acronym for Mizunderstood Mondays.

Each week, Tre and close friend/brother, producer MIzunderstood Ru, are pledging to drop a new heat rock for all their old and new fans alike.

Do You want my suggestion?

Hop on this wave now, and ride out with the team.
