Post Malone Sees Similarities Between Kanye West & Justin Bieber

2018-08-08 — news

In an interview set to air during the August 8 episode of Complex x Fuse, chart-topping artist Post Malone shares the similarities he sees between Justin Bieber and Kanye West -- both of whom he's worked with -- while sampling intensely spicy hot sauce."They're so driven," he says. "They complete a task ... start something, they're going to finish it."He also shared the feeling he had hitting a stadium stage (supporting Bieber) for the first time."I was so nervous, and it never got better," he says, noting he felt the same dread each night. "It's the same feeling ... it's terrifying."Also on the episode, Jamie Foxx shares celebrity stories, Meek Mill talks sneakers, Producer Mike Will Made-It reveals how he made Beyonce’s hit single, “Formation, and much more.Catch Complex x Fuse on Wednesday (August 8) at 11 p.m.

ET/PT on Fuse.
