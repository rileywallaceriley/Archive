Things Get Gross In Aaron Cohen & Jarren Benton’s 'Yeezy 350' Video

2020-01-29 — news

Fresh off of the release of Aaron Cohen's "Street Meat” single featuring Trizz and Kyle Bent, he and frequent collaborator Jarren Benton have dropped a visual for Cohen’s Raw Every Day standout “Yeezy 350," which is produced by DJ J Hart,Warning: it isn’t for anyone with a weak stomach.Directed by Sonya Goddy, the video puts a literal spin on the album’s theme. As the two spit their verses, actors Brianna O’Reilly, Kian Freitas, Sarah Schultz and Mia Darrow (Cohen’s girlfriend) consume raw meat.No surprise at all, it causes them to vomit uncontrollably.

It comes to a climax when Darrow projectile vomits all over Cohen’s face.In an interview with HipHopDX last August, Cohen hinted at a collaborative project with Benton.“Working with Jarren comes naturally,” he said. “We both have aggressive styles. We both have bars.

We both don’t give a shit about trends We also just get along well as human beings. We’re both dedicated to our craft, and neither of us are pretentious egomaniacs ... [we also] share a manager now, Max Alcabes, which makes working together even easier.”Stream the “Yeezy 350" video above.
