Bubbing LA transplant Peter $un just dropped the official music video for "Things Can't Stay The Same," the standout single from his 2022 EP&nbsp;Phone Calls Gimme Anxiety&nbsp;on Red Bull Records/Mind of a Genius Records in 2022.&nbsp;

The EP is an introspective reflection of his life experiences, with a recurring theme of accepting the need for growth and moving on from past pain.

Through his authentic and relatable lyrics, Peter $un acknowledges the need for change, singing, "I've got to change, get my shit together." The video's moody visuals and trippy edits are well-matched with the breathy chorus and twinkling keys, inviting viewers into the world of Peter $un as he closes this chapter of his life and looks ahead to the future.

"This release of the "Things Can't Stay The Same" music video marks the end of the 'Phone Calls Give Me Anxiety' phase of music.

The video indicates the halfway point to the next evolution of my music.

Think of it as a break up from the old constructs of conciseness in love & life in my last release.

This exodus frees me to explore life in its most uninhibited form on these next new releases.

I'm so excited about where we are, but more excited for what's to come."&nbsp;

peter $un
