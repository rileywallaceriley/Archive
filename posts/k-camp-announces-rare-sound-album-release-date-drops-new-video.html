K Camp Announces "RARE Sound" Album Release Date & Drops New Video

2018-10-11 — news

After unveiling cover art on September 20, K Camp’s RARE Sound LP now has an official release date and preorder. He first plugged the album this past May via Twitter after resolving what he described as “bad label situation.” He kept his followers excited, dropping a surprise 5-track EP this past July called This Is For You.“Tell them folks the GOAT back,” he wrote on Twitter last month.

The LP is set to feature Lil Durk, Wiz Khalifa, UnoTheActivist, Nessly, as well as in-house RARE Sound artists TrueStory Gee and GENIUS — who appears on the album’s newly released first single (and video) "Can't Go Home.” The LP also appears to include the single “Can’t Get Enough,” which appeared on This For You.https://youtu.be/Ci9ry9AmVFYK Camp is keeping the production all in-house, with Bobby Kritical, PionEars, Euro, and others crafting the project’s 16-tracks.An official tracklist has yet to surface. The album is available everywhere November 2.[apple_news_ad type="standard"]
