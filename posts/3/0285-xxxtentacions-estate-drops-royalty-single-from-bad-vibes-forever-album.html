XXXTENTACION’s Estate Drops "Royalty" Single From “Bad Vibes Forever” Album

2019-07-19 — news

The late XXXTENTACION flexes his Jamaican heritage on the new posthumous single, “Royalty.”Produced by past X collaborator JonFX ("Jocelyn Flores") — who rose to notoriety after crafting Gyptian’s smash "Hold Yuh" — the single is the first taste of the upcoming Bad Vibes Forever LP, which is dropping soon via Bad Vibes Forever/EMPIRE.Dipped in X’s melodic flow, the song features verses from Ky-Mani Marley, Vybz Kartel and Stefflon Don.https://youtu.be/Ka0NOe2PwCMThe single follows the trailer for an upcoming documentary on the slain rapper, which his estate released last month.X narrates the clip (which has racked up over 6 million plays) himself and states, "This is the story.
