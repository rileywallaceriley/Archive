Conway The Machine & Trillmatic Drop ”Draco” Video

2019-06-10 — news

Griselda’s own Conway The Machine comes through with a brand new visual for “Draco” produced by Mephux — a song which appears on his collaborative EP with the Trillmatic Brand, Organized Grime.The video was shot in Dorchester, Massachusetts, by Myster DL for Ill Mannered Films — who tells HipHopDX that he also has new visuals by Benny The Butcher on the docket.“Conway wants me to come to Buffalo for a mess of videos,” Myster DL says.It’s been a busy month for Griselda, who also dropped their long-awaited collaboration with DJ Premier, “Headlines.” The imprint’s first official Shady Records project, WWCD, is due out this summer.Watch Conway and Trillmatic’s “Draco” above.[apple_news_ad type="standard”]
