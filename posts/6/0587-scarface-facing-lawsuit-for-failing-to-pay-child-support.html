Scarface Facing Lawsuit For Failing To Pay Child Support

2018-08-21 — news

According to an exclusive report by Bossip, Hip Hop Icon Scarface is once again in hot water for failing to pay his child support.

The Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton is currently suing him on behalf of his child's mother Lisa Varner -- seeking garnished wages and a raised monthly payment.Scarface was first sued by Varner in 2011 to prove that he is, in fact, the father of the child to establish child support.

He was then ordered to pay $148 a month and an additional $30 for medical expenses.He is $6,728 in arrears, according to court papers.The latest suit isn't Facemob's first brush with child support drama.

He gave a statement to AllHipHop to correct what he described as misinformation about his case.“The amount reported that was owed is inaccurate, and I had no knowledge about there being an arrest order for me," he said.To date, Scarface has not responded to the latest suit but is due in court later this month.
