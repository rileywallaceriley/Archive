Hip Hop Royalty Attends N.O.R.E’s “5E” Listening Party

2018-07-26 — news

N.O.R.E. hosted a listening party for his new album 5E on Wednesday night (July 25).

Taking place at Jue Lan Club in New York City, some of Hip Hop’s most respected MCs came through to show their support.25 lucky fans — and one guest each — were given access to the exclusive event, which was hosted by Mass Appeal.

Some of the notable artists in attendance were N.O.R.E.'s longtime partner-in-rhyme Capone, Nas, AZ, Fat Joe, Mysonne, Styles P, Ja Rule, Raekwon, Fabolous, Busta Rhymes, Maino, Dave East and Rakim.5E, the Drink Champs co-host’s first album in five years, includes guest appearances by Fat Joe, Wyclef Jean, Jadakiss, Tory Lanez, Fabolous, Pharrell and The-Dream.

The first single, “Don’t Know.” is featured on the soundtrack for Madden 19.The album drops on Friday (July 27) via Mass Appeal.
