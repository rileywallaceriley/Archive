#DXCLUSIVE: Pounds Drops “Equifax” Video & Talks Working With Buckwild

2019-07-02 — news

Rochester, New York, rapper/producer Pounds released a new visual for “Equifax” — which appears on his Blue Gardenia EP — on Tuesday (July 2).

The video is an appetizer sure to keep fans on edge as they wait for the two projects he has dropping later this summer.In July, he’s slated to release The T.O.N.Y. EP, which will feature bars from the likes of Bodega Bamz, Crimeapple, and Flee Lord — with production from Eto and Spittzwell.

In August, he’ll follow that up with Trafficante — an LP produced entirely by iconic producer Buckwild, whose expansive catalogue includes work with rap icons The Notorious B.I.G and Big L.The creation of Trafficante is something Pounds tells HipHopDX was quite humbling.“Honestly, working with Buckwild was just surreal.

Doing a project with a producer who had such an impact on hip hop is an honor and indescribable,” he explains.“Buck is a legend with more hits under his belt than the iceman ... we definitely got some shit for y’all.”Check out Pounds’ “Equifax” video above.
