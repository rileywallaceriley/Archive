POLO Teams Up With Myles Anthony For New Single From 'Trapcouver'

Sat, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Frequently featured producer, Myles Anthony, and his longtime friend—Surrey, British Columbia, native—POLO.

Surrey, B.C. is one of the rougher areas of the lower mainland. "The Strip," has been profiled on national news circuits as a hotspot for drugs and crime, and high-profile busts and raids.

Shaped by the environment POLO's distinctive cadence and flow ooze authenticity; coupled with the much-hailed production of Myles, whose worked with the likes as one-half of the platinum selling VA, The Clipse—and more recently president of Kanye West's GOOD Music imprint—his story shines through. "Truth Be Told is the first release from the upcoming Trapcouver mixtape, entirely produced by Myles.

Aside from an accessible shining a different spotlight on the world's most desirable city to live, it will be an opportunity for the world to get up POLO.

Check out the new song below, and if you're feeling it, mash the share button. https://soundcloud.com/polopacino/truth-be-told-prod-by-myles-anthony
