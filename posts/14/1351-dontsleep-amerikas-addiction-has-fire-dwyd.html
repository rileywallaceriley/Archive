Amerikas Addiction--a dope new hip-hop duo consisting of David Kelly and producer Joshua Scarver-- have a new EP entitled The Prelude To which has been in heavy rotation in Atlanta.

Collaborating with DJ 23 from the multi-platinum producer Drumma Boy's "Drum Squad DJ's," they gave the release ample mass-appeal, with a side of some serious star potential.

They are now gearing up to release another EP, Four, which is produced by RMB Justize from Wiz Khalifa's Taylor Gang crew.

No biggie.

The duo also have their lifestyle brand, #DoWhatYouDesire aka #DWYD and have also been extras on the hit TV show "Empire.'' With some serious prospects and a high profile performance at Summerfest--the world's largest music festival--under their belt, the guys are clearly on the right trajectory.
