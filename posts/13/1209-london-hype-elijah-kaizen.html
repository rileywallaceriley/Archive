"There's no glory in keeping it real -- most people can't handle the truth." That's the type of gems you're going to get from London, UK, rapper Elijah Kaizen.

He's an artist's artist, who seems to exhibit the mindset that helped to build this culture initially -- and after you watch his mini video EPK, you'll understand what I mean by that.

What he lacks in flashy gimmicks, he more than makes up for with a refreshing dose of realistic insight and knowledge of self.

His recently released project, Cold Winter, blends soulful beats with dope lyricism and is a must listen.

Hip-hop is truly universal; don't sleep on this heat from across the pond.
