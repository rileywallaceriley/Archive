#DXCLUSIVE: Angelo King Connects With Waka Flocka Flame For “NO OFF SEASON” Video

2019-07-19 — news

Rotterdam, Netherlands artist Angelo King has teamed with Waka Flocka Flame for “NO OFF SEASON” — a street anthem off of his upcoming LP Chase.Drenched in hard-hitting production and brimming with motivational undertones, the Sony Music signee notes that the song immediately spoke to the Brick Squad rapper when they first connected.“We hit the studio, and this was the first song I played him,” he explained via a statement. “He didn’t want to hear nothing else.”Shot in Georgia, the visual sees the two showing mutual respect for each other’s hometowns.

Waka is seen donning a Feyenoord Rotterdam jersey while Angelo sports an Atlanta Hawks Jersey.The song is set to appear on Angelo’s highly anticipated double album Chase — a project named after his son.

With his background being both Dancehall and Hip Hop, the eclectic release will also feature Stefflon Don, Gyptian, and 808 Mafia.Watch Angelo King and Waka Flocka’s “NO OFF SEASON” above.
