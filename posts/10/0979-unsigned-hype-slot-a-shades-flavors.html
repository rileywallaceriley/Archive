Unsigned Hype: Slot-A – Shades & Flavors

Sun, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

Recently, windy city producer Slot-A, released his first remix project, Shades & Flavors.

Shades & Flavors features thirteen remixed and reproduced songs from artists like Kevin Gates to classic records from Camp Lo.

Slot, whose produced for the likes of for Add-2, LEP, Maino, Chuuwee, Jamall Bufford, spent a lot of time on the sequencing this one-of-a-kind aural experience.

Shades & Flavors is the first instalment of a 3 part remix project that will be coming soon.

Slot is also a DJ – you can catch his THHP Mix Show every Saturday on WLUW 88.7FM Chicago or wluw.org.
