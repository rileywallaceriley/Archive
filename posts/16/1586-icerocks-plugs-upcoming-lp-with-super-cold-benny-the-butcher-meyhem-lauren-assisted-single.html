IceRocks Plugs Upcoming LP With Super Cold Benny The Butcher & Meyhem Lauren Assisted Single

Thu, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

IceRocks has been immersing himself in the new wave of underground Hip Hop currently bubbling over — racking up credits with Griselda affiliates Flee Lord and Elcamino, Benny The Butcher, G4Jag, and more.

Not to mention joint projects, like his Cashmere EP with F...

That's Delicious co-star and Action Bronson affiliate Meyhem Lauren. 

However, he's eyeing the next level with his new producer LP dropping next month.&nbsp;

Crossing the Rubicon is poised to be a big deal for the Queen native, with verses from underground, new school royalty like Flee Lord, Madhattan, Ren Thomas, Rome Streetz, Rick Hyde, UFO Fev, Heem, Elcamino and Skrewtape to name a few.

While we patiently wait for the March 5th release date, Ice drops off the first single from the project — featuring Benny The Butcher and Meyhem Lauren.

Stream IceRock'sIceRock's "Red Presidents" below, and if you've been sleeping on his production and want to catch up, start with last Summer's Kitchen Chronicles collaborative EP with Madhatten.
