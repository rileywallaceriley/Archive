#DXCLUSIVE: G. Huff & Lena Jackson Connect With M.O.P For ‘Another Dead Rapper’ Remix

2020-02-21 — news

Continuing to support their 2019 collaborative LP Grace and Alchemy, Midwest MC G. Huff and Southern spitter Lena Jackson release a remix for the standout “Another Dead Rapper” featuring the iconic Brownsville duo M.O.P.With Statik Selektah adding his spin to the iLLBrown produced song, the remix doesn’t just build on the aggressive energy of the original — it cranks the dial.“The energy and aggression were already apparent on the original, so it’s only right the masters of aggression came through to enhance this joint and make it a whole other experience,” Lena Jackson said via statement.Huff adds, “‘Another Dead Rapper’ was one of those pivotal album cuts and people I respect like DJ Premier spun it heavily ... we wanted to get M.O.P because there is no greater duo with their level of chemistry.

Spitting alongside them proves we’re an elite rap duo ourselves.”Stream G. Huff and Lena Jackson’s “Another Dead Rapper” featuring M.O.P above.

Grace and Alchemy is available now on all streaming networks.
