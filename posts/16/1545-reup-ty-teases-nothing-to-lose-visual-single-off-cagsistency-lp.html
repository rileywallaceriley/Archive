ReUp Ty Teases "Nothing To Lose" Visual/Single Off Cagsistency LP

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

As we reported a few weeks ago, Chicago MC, ReUp Ty is dropping a nine-song LP called Cagsistency, produced by James Cagne — set to drop this quarter.

With the haunting first single "All I Need," with its gritty visual treatment still in rotation, a new video has just dropped -- teasing the next single and showing ReUp and James in the studio.

While sipping and nodding his head to James's heavy production, we get a chance to see ReUp in his element -- in the studio.

After being played an instrumental that he's obviously feeling, we're treated to the resulting single "Nothing To Lose" -- presented against a studio backdrop, and some cool lights.

With this super catchy track, we're even more geeked to get our hands on the full release and dig in.
