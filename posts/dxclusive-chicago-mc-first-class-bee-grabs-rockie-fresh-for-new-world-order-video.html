#DXCLUSIVE: Chicago MC First Class Bee Grabs Rockie Fresh For 'New World Order' Video

2020-02-03 — news

Rapper First Class Bee, a Chicago-based entrepreneur turned entertainer, tapped Rostrum Records spitter Rockie Fresh for his new DJ O-Zone-produced single "New World Order."The track, which sees the two trading luxury raps, came about organically."In all honesty, the record came together pretty smoothly," Bee tells HipHopDX. "DJ O-Zone [who has produced for the likes of Chance The Rapper and Mobb Deep] texted me ... he said he made a beat and wanted to put me in the studio with Rockie to see what comes about. [This song] was the outcome of that session."Stream the video for First Class Bee and Rockie Fresh's "New World Order" above. Fans can also catch First Class Bee opening up for Griselda during the Chicago stop of their WWCD Tour on February 28.[apple_news_ad type="standard"]
