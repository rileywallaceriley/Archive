"As long as you're happy -- that's all that matters." Those are the first words we hear on the new mini-movie by Ann Arbor, MI, rapper Oneup2Lovett, whose been featured here before.

It's a 14-minute journey through the eyes of the Michigan-native.

He shares his thoughts, gives insight, and provides you with a dope amidst a lush soundtrack.

The video starts with a reflective monologue that describes not only his personal demons -- but the ills that seem to be engulphing communities and plaguing the minds of the youth.

Amidst night time visuals, there is an intense build up that works to hammer home the internal struggle the rapper deals with.

This isn't a music video drawn out; although it does have a few prominent songs features -- but rather, an atmospheric blend of moody sounds that serve as more of a dope score than anything else.

Look, man; we've been supporting Oneup2Lovett for a minute.

The man has the skill, and "TRU" is another notch for him.

Check it out below -- and also check out the super bumping "Music," in case you missed it.
