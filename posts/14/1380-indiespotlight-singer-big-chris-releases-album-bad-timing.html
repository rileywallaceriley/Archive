UK based singer Big Chris is a pleasant surprise; His last single "F The World" dropped over the summer , but now his new record is out and features a ton of quality content, with a fresh dose of old-school R&B sensibility.

Sitting at eight tracks in length--and featuring "F The World" as a closer--he explores solid songwriting and traverses quality and cohesive production on standouts like "Shoosh," the tile track, and "Home Tonight." At points, it has a late 90s feeling to it; it's a good balance.

Recorded by Big Chris and John Robinson at Clique Studios, London and Miami Live, Miami.

The album mix was finalised by Mixbytrip at Circle House Miami.

Inspirations of The Dream, Mike Posner and R Kelly can be heard throughout.

Definitely put this project on your weekly playlist if you're down for some quality R&B; Big Chris has something special here.
