Change Your Mindset: The Music Of Scotty Chams

Tue, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

In a shaky political climate and a world that's seemingly overrun by corporate and media influence, voice like that of Scotty Chams becomes a crucial balance in a sea of trapped-out music riding a wave of commercialism. "This project is mostly old school hip hop beats — but the style is unique and modern," he tells AAHH about his latest release, System Failure. "It's mostly talking about government corruption, different perspectives, a mindset to succeed, and my opinion on a lot of what's going on in our culture." With over 3 million plays as an artist —which is impressive — the founder of "We The 9" has been building a steady buzz since he first dropped two years ago. "My music is based on following your passion and positivity — similar to Logic's vision.

I also talk a lot about spirituality, reality, and understanding." A fresh welcomed addition to the game; check out some of Scotty's releases, below.
