Uncle Murda's Tekashi 6ix9ine Diss Wishes For His Jail Rape

2019-02-25 — news

It appears that a savage Tekashi 6ix9ine diss record by Uncle Murda could see the light of day, assuming the social interest is there.“I never tried this before 30k comments and I’m dropping this,” the G-Unit rapper promised via Instagram on Sunday (February 24).

In the short — but absolutely potent — preview, he not only acknowledges the fact that he’s collaborated with the jailed rapper (on “Get The Strap”), but that he has no qualms with kicking him while he’s down.Over the reworked instrumental of Ice Cube’s “Today Was A Good Day,” he raps, “Hope he get raped while they got him in PC by a big black nigga that look like Adebisi/I’m kicking him while he’s down, yeah fuck that rat nigga/Didn’t you do a song with him?/Fuck that trap nigga.”Elsewhere in the clip, he calls 69’s baby mother Sara Molina a slut, and — as the clip abruptly ends — seems to begin eluding the possible danger his mother and child could be in.Check out the full snippet, below.
