#DXCLUSIVE: Da Flyy Hooligan & CRIMEAPPLE Connect For Oh No-Produced ‘FLYYAPPLE’

2020-02-14 — news

New Jersey's CRIMEAPPLE and UK spitter Da Flyy Hooligan have debuted a Ringz Ov Saturn-directed visual for their collaborative single “FLYYAPPLE.”Produced by Oh No, fresh off the release of The Professionals album alongside his brother Madlib, the song promises to be the first of many from the new duo, who met backstage during the 2019 Hip Hop Kemp Festival in Central Europe.“This is just the beginning, B,” Hooligan tells HipHopDX.CRIMEAPPLE adds, “We got more in the stash!”Stream Da Flyy Hooligan and CRIMEAPPLE’s Oh No-produced “FLYYAPPLE” above.
