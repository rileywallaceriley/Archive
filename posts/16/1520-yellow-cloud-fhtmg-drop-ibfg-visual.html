Yellow Cloud & FHTMG Drop "IBFG" Visual

Fri, — aboveaveragehiphop.com

DMV camps Yellow Cloud (AAHH mainstays) and Forest Hills Tenleytown Music Group (FHTMG), have once again connected for their brand new banger, "IBFG," which they've dropped off with a dope new video that we've been bumping on repeat all day.

Related: Yellow Cloud & FHTMG Connect For ‘Major Key’

Over the bouncy instrumental with its lo-fi sample and booming bass, Treezus (of Yellow Cloud) and Abu Rahss trade some dope wordplay, and braggadocios Hip Hop shit!

The Treelag-produced visual treatment maintains an intriguing super eight camera aesthetic with its glitchy transitions and sporadic effects that almost feel like a malfunctioning security camera.

The Handycam aura gives it a really dope vibe. "I been feeling good, I been feeling great," the chorus echoes before adding that they've been chilling and smoking backwoods.

The overall "living my best life" schtick is infectious; it creates an earworm that begs for playlist placement.
