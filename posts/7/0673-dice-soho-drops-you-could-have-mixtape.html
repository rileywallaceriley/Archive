Dice Soho Drops "You Could Have" Mixtape

2018-05-18 — news

After a few weeks of hype, 22-year-old Houston native — and Mike Dean protege — Dice Soho followed through and dropped off the follow-up to 2016’s 0 Degrees.Titled You Could Have, the mixtape sits at 13 songs, which include previously released singles “SSP” featuring Ty Dolla $ign and Desiigner, “Need More” featuring PnB Rock, “Giraffe,” and the 2016 track “Ripped Jeans.”Executively produced by Mike Dean, the “Just Watch” rapper also enlists Wiz Khalifa, Kap G, Sonny Digital, Blac Youngsta, and Jersey upstart Dougie F.Check the stream and tracklist for Dice Soho’s You Could Have, below.https://open.spotify.com/album/4fl87XCbX322fdfUjECoz4?si=FmdQFm6VQmukklz8JoymXgTracklist1. “She Will” featuring Vory2. “SSP” featuring Ty Dolla $ign and Desiigner3. “Time It Is”4. “Either Way” featuring Wiz Khalifa5. “Giraffe”6. “Watered Down”7. “Need More” featuring PnB Rock8. “Switch” featuring Dougie F9. “Came a Long Way” featuring Kap G10. “Snowflake Emoji”11. “Orders” featuring Sonny Digital12. “Yin” featuring Blac Youngsta13. “Ripped Jeans”
