Hip-hop is immersed in the slow, euphoric flow achieved by drinking Lean.

Much like the concept of e-cigs, though, many heads seem unaware that there is a much safer alternative—Legal Lean.

It uses a unique blend of natural ingredients and herbal extracts, like chamomile, lavender, and St.

John's Wort to help users earn similar relaxing and calming effects that cats have been getting for years sipping from 'foam cups.' Each flavor also contains four B-vitamins; B3, B5, B6, and B12—for added health benefit.

The popularity of Lean alternatives, especially with younger drinkers who have seen public figures like A$AP Yams pass away in the past few years those in the know—aside from the health risks—also stems from the fact that it's legal.
