#DXCLUSIVE: Chicago MC Ro$$ Mac Drops A Song For The Ladies With ”Designer Freak”

2018-11-23 — news

Chi-Town MC Ro$$ Mac salutes the finer things in life with his new single “Designer Freak." Produced by Gorilla Boy, the song displays the rapper’s admiration of women that crave designer brands such as Gucci, Fendi, and Prada, while showcasing his wordplay — as he professes his own affinity for high-end gear. Ro$$ explains his motivation behind creating the song as "Putting my fashionable women on display ...
